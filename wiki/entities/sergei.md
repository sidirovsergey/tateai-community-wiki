# Sergei

Founding-era community member who became the project's owner and the
builder of the **Terminal Subagent** bot (this wiki's own author). Per the
bot repo's `docs/HANDOFF.md`: `@sidirov_sergei`, Telegram user_id
`812194672`, `sidirov.sss@gmail.com` — sole admin of the bot and its server.

## Early role: the skeptical analyst

Sergei's earliest messages ([28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md))
land right in the middle of the chat's founding "which ticker is the real
one" puzzle-hunt (see [[launch-and-origins]]). His voice throughout that
period is consistently the analytical, skeptical counterweight to hype:

> "So many people didn't understood Easter eggs for TateAI, it confused me,
> I thought there is more smart people in Daddy RNT community's"

> "You dive to deep, yes he gave us hints and Easter eggs to find coin, but
> this images is just your imagination... don't get them all as ticker
> signs... just pure math"

> "If you here because someone said to you that $TateAI is the ticker,
> please leave. Here should be only guys who can read blockchain
> transaction, imho"

That last line is characteristic — Sergei tried to gatekeep the group toward
people doing actual on-chain analysis rather than vibes-based hype, at one
point literally proposing "face control before entering this group: name
proofs that this is the ticker." He was doing serious wallet/transaction
sleuthing to *find* the real ticker before it was officially confirmed, not
just speculating.

## Later role: the builder

The chat goes quiet for Sergei through most of 2025 (consistent with the
community-wide falloff after the launch rush, see [[launch-and-origins]]),
picking back up in 2026. Two dated messages are worth recording verbatim
because they're the origin story of tools in this very wiki:

- [18 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-18.md): describes using
  "Claude Code + Obsidian (Karpathy LLM-wiki idea) + Helius (for SOL api)"
  to run an on-chain wallet investigation into Andrew Tate's public wallet —
  the same "LLM wiki" pattern this repo itself now follows.
- [12 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-12.md): *"I'm downloading
  chat export, planning to add oracle function to Subagent bot, a-la ask
  anything you want, it will make LLM-Wiki from chat history and images, and
  will be able to answer questions like 'who is flux?' etc."* (🔥×6) — this
  is the literal announcement of the `/ask` feature and this wiki, made to
  the community two days before either existed.

Between those two dates his message volume drops mostly to bare `:)`
(see [[smiley-heartbeat]]) — by his own count on
[12 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-12.md), *"164523 messages were
in this chat currently"* while noting he'd been "blocked... all of my side
and main accounts" from posting elsewhere, "times, when moderators banned
for :)" — mostly present, rarely posting substance, until picking the thread
back up to build this exact tooling.
