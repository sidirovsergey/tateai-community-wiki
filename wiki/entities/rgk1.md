# rgk1

One of the chat's highest-volume posters and its most consistent in-character
persona: a "be cool, hold the line" motivational-philosopher voice that runs
almost unbroken from the earliest days through at least early 2025.

## Voice, phase 1 — the zen holder (Nov 2024)

The signature move is a catchphrase — **"Be cool 😎🆒"** — attached to
cosmic-scale metaphors about holding through volatility:

> "Andrew is the SUN, TateAI is the earth. He will shine the light on us
> when he is ready... Be COOL."
> — [9 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-09.md)

> "No top holders have sold. Because they have connections and info...
> Follow the winners... We are the cool kids on the block. Be cool, work and
> trust the process. 🆒😎"
> — [9 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-09.md)

> "There are no loses til you sell. The coins stay in your bag until you
> remove them. Choose wisely grasshopper. Be Cool 😎"
> — [11 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-11.md)

The "grasshopper" address recurs — a deliberate kung-fu-master-dispensing-
wisdom affect, paired with a steady stream of reaction gifs.

## Voice, phase 2 — the AI-agent-narrative poet (early 2025)

By [January 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-02.md), during the chat's
secondary activity wave tied to the broader crypto "AI agent" narrative (see
[[launch-and-origins]] for the volume data), rgk1's register shifts to dense,
riddle-like prose essays about AI agents, still tightly on-brand for the
project:

> "Is Andrew the General manager and Terminal the Head Coach? Then TateAI is
> the high scorer and MVP of this season?"

> "The blockchain is more than just a technological innovation; it's a
> memetic revolution."

> "Our dreams are the alpha version of reality; diamond hand through the
> fail whale to claim your 100x on the dex screener."

This later material is also the chat's densest source of explicit
diamond-hands rhetoric — see [[diamond-hands]].
