# Arthur

A pure launch-rush phenomenon: one of the chat's very first posters (first
message [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md) at 02:05, within
hours of the group's founding) and one of its highest-volume, but confined
almost entirely to the first seven weeks. His last recorded message is [17
Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-17.md) — after that he simply
doesn't appear again in the sampled material, unlike long-haul holders such
as [[jame_s]] who are still posting in 2026.

## Sheer volume of memes

A large fraction of Arthur's message count is not text at all — long,
unbroken runs of `[media:gif]` and `[media:sticker]` posts, sometimes 10-15
in a row within the same minute, functioning as a kind of running visual
commentary track on the chat. Text messages are short bursts: a
"Buahahahahaha" laugh, "kek," "lol," or a raid-command hashtag
(`/RAID_TIME`, `/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`, `/TOP_COMMENT_LFG`) repeated
five or ten times in immediate succession. He is bilingual, dropping into
French mid-conversation:

> "Ne le partage pas pour l'instant nigger" ("Don't share it yet")
> — [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)

> "Yes, t'es au top mec 🍻" ("you're the best, man")
> — [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)

## The recurring bit: interrogating the bot as "Real Nigger Tate"

Arthur's most distinctive recurring behavior is directly addressing a chat
bot he calls `@TateAI_Bot` as though it were "Real Nigger Tate" (a riff on
the rival `$RNT` ticker's own backronym — see
[[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] for how tangled this chat's Tate-adjacent
names get) and demanding it perform for the room:

> "@TateAI_Bot Yo real nigger Tate. Why patience is a virtue? / Explain this
> to your nigger soldiers out there"
> — [5 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md) (🔥×1)

> "Real nigger Tate roast this faggot bot @tate_terminalbot"
> — [7 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-07.md)

He also repeats a "patience" catchphrase across the earliest days, distinct
from but parallel to [[rgk1]]'s "Be cool":

> "Be patient my frens / Patient is the name of the game"
> — [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)

He is also visibly involved in raid logistics and chat moderation-adjacent
bot commands (`/mute`, `/ban`, `/unban`, `/filter`, `/config`) throughout
early November — whether this reflects an actual admin role or just heavy
use of a member-facing moderation bot isn't clear from message content
alone; at least once he's on the receiving end of a mute himself ("Thanks
for the unmute my G," [29 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)), which argues against him
being an admin at that point.

## The disappearance

Arthur's very last messages, on [17 Dec
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-17.md), are not about `$TateAi` at all
— they're inviting the chat to a different Telegram group for a token
called "Shyguy" ("Join Shyguy nigga," "After shyguy when we all printed we
won't need Tate to send tateai we do it ourselves"). He is not seen again
in the sampled material after that date.
