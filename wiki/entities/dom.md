# Dom

A striking data point rather than a distinct personality: every one of
Dom's 2,134 messages falls between
[29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md) and
[11 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-11.md) — thirteen days, all of
it inside the chat's single most frantic stretch (see the overview's "70%
of everything in the first three weeks" note). That's an average of over
160 messages a day. He never appears again anywhere in the raw material
after 11 Nov 2024.

## Voice: undiluted launch hype

Read on its own, Dom's content is not distinguishable from the general
crowd noise of that fortnight — raid calls, jeet-shaming, SOL-begging,
FOMO, constant slurs, caps-lock excitement. What makes him worth a page is
volume and relentlessness, not a unique angle:

> "CAN SOMEONE LEND ME SOL?????" / "PLEASE" / "WILL GIVE YOU MY GIRL"
> — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

> "Thanks boys for keeping me confident at those dip because I just joined
> earlier today❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️I nearly sold because of saw Chris message"
> — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

That message is a small window into how people arrived in these first
days: Dom says a YouTuber named Chris's channel brought him in, then Chris
himself apparently got "shaken out" and tried to talk people out of
holding — Dom stayed.

> "This is the biggest cult I've ever been a part of"
> — [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)

> "WE ARE THE OG MFERS" / "REMEMBER OUR NAMES"
> — [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)

Occasional German phrases ("Guten Morgen G," "Bin um 2 pennen") suggest a
German-speaking background, same as several other regulars from this era.
