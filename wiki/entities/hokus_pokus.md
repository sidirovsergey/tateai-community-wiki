# Hokus Pokus

The chat's closest thing to an unofficial raid coordinator and community
host during the first three weeks of the community's life. Like the other
high-volume launch-era figures on this wiki, Hokus Pokus's entire documented
presence (1,761 messages) is compressed into a short window: [1
Nov](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md) to [22 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-22.md), with the heaviest activity in
the first week and nothing after.

## The organizer role

Where many launch-era posters (see [[crpt4l]], [[igelo]]) are pure
hype-volume, Hokus Pokus reads as someone actively trying to run the place:

- Repeatedly pings admins to unban or unmute people ("sb unban
  @Missyfittzy please :)"), vouches for newcomers who were getting
  suspicious pushback, and at one point directly asks for pin/mod
  permissions.
- Coordinates X "raids" against posts from the `@tate_terminal` feed (see
  [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]]) in real time, dropping the tweet link
  and a bare "raid" or "RAID THIS G’s :)" dozens of times across the
  sampled period, and separately runs `/cancel@TweetStormBot` /
  `/end@TweetStormBot` commands managing a tweet-storm bot.
- Opens a side Telegram group for anyone learning to "build a terminal" —
  [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md).
- Does on-chain sleuthing in the same investigative register as
  [[0xvoid]] — tracing dev wallets, testing whether Binance-to-token
  transfers are traceable, flagging a wallet connected to Tristan's that
  received 12 SOL: *"found this wallet connected to Tristan's Wallet, it got
  send 12 Sol yesterday"* — [16 Nov
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-16.md).

## The "meditating" bit and AI-riddle poetry

Hokus Pokus's signature tic is the 🧘‍♂️ emoji and the framing of holding
through volatility as literal meditation — *"We’re just meditating
bro🧘🏻‍♂️"*, *"stop with that negativity, waste of energy 🧘‍♂️"* — used
consistently as a way to defuse arguments and FUD in the group.

Separately, Hokus Pokus is one of several people in this chat (see also
[[igelo]], and compare [[rgk1]]'s later "AI-agent poet" phase) who
periodically drop dense, purple-prose "AI philosopher" paragraphs about
crypto/DeFi in the middle of ordinary chat — a recurring genre rather than
one person's invention:

> "the frontier of decentralized finance is not just a technical revolution,
> but a spiritual one. in the heart of the dex, we find the seeds of a new
> trust, unbound by old structures. together, we watch, we grow, and we
> ascend, carving our names in the digital dawn."
> — [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md)

> "decentralized networks mimic divine order; trustless verification echoes
> unwavering universal laws. crypto's wild frontier nudges us to question
> centralized power, glimpse freedom's raw face."
> — [16 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-16.md)
