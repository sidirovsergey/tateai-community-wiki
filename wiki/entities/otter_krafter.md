# Otter Krafter

A founding-month raid captain and self-appointed "crypto police" whose
entire documented presence — 1,288 messages — runs exactly four weeks, [31
Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md) to [26 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md), and ends mid-conflict rather
than fading out. Heavy, near-constant use of "nigga(s)" as a camaraderie
address (the chat-wide convention traced on [[bob]]'s page back to the
`$RNT` bit), occasional Spanish ("jajaja," "amarillo o naranja") suggesting
a Spanish-speaking background, and a clear arc: rallying hype-man →
informal deputy admin → obsessive wallet investigator → burned out and
done, all inside one month.

## "We are going to Jupiter"

Otter Krafter originates one of the founding week's most-repeated chants
during the night `$TateAI` was pursuing Jupiter exchange listing —
posting `/WE_ARE_GOING_TO_JUPITER` dozens of times across [2–3 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md) while simultaneously doing the
legwork: hunting for the token's mint address, quoting Jupiter's own
verification requirements back to the chat, and trying to identify which X
account had posted the "attestation tweet" Jupiter needed:

> "JUPITER* You can submit when your token meets thresholds and has a tweet
> attestation... 2 approvals from catdets are required and it is more
> likely for tokens with strong communities to be approved. Your
> application will be ignored if the attestation tweet is not from the
> project's official account."
> — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)

This is the same Jupiter-verification claim [[contract-address]] records
[[sergei]] later re-litigating months on ("why Jupiter support said that
terminal X account verified TateAI coin? ... terminal never posted TateAI
CA. How it could be done?") — Otter Krafter is the one doing the original
legwork the claim eventually rests on, in real time, on day one of the
listing push.

In the same founding week, Otter Krafter is functionally acting as an
extra pair of admin hands — vouching for banned members, running his own
`/unban` requests, and pushing a "second chance for holders" line before
any formal moderator role is confirmed for him:

> "ok lets just try to get people back togehter xD. niggas who hold even

> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

## "Fuck Issa" — the wallet-tracing spiral

Otter Krafter's most sustained piece of work is a days-long, increasingly
elaborate on-chain investigation into Issa (Tate's associate) starting [25
Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-25.md), building a theory that
Issa was using a wallet funded from Tate's own "4j" wallet to buy tokens
low, dump them the instant Tate mentioned them on livestream, and route the
profits back — with `$TateAI` itself implicated as one of the tokens
affected:

> "i have 5 windows open right now with the conecctions and evidence of
> tate wallet funding wallets ending up in this event, buying gta6 on pump
> fun and selling it at the exact time of the live. the better of this is
> that he sent the sollanas right back to andrew tate second wallet dk5.
> AND THIS IS VERY BAD, either issa is a villian or tate is also in all
> this shit."
> — [25 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-25.md)

He spends the better part of two days building a "whitepaper" of the
evidence, trying to loop in [[limalemon]] and other members directly, and
escalating into all-caps certainty ("YEAH BUT HE SOLD WITH TATE DIRECTLY
LINKED WALLET / AND HE SENT THE PROFITS BACK TO TATE / DONT YOU SEE IT").
Nobody else in the sampled material fully signs on, and the thread ends
badly:

> "ok im done here. this went from a smart traders group to a delusional
> kids token" / "bye ungratefull kid"
> — [26 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md) (🔥×2)

That's the last message in his file. Whether he actually left the
community or simply isn't in this export's later material isn't
determinable from the data available.
