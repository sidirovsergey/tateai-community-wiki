# Daniel R

One of the chat's most consistent long-haul presences, full stop. First
message [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md), and the raw
material's last entry for this sender is a bare ":)" on [14 Jul
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-14.md) — within days of the export
itself. Unlike most launch-era heavy hitters who either vanish (see
[[dom]], [[arthur]]) or taper into occasional check-ins (see [[i_m_lucky]],
[[svyatoslav]]), Daniel R's presence barely thins: after an inventive,
hands-on launch month, he settles into one of the chat's most reliable
daily "GM :)" ritual-posters, sustained for well over a year, with genuine
personal-life disclosure woven through it.

## The promo-video maker who helped spread "rain dance"

Where most launch-week members raided with text and screenshots, Daniel R
brought editing skills. He produces and posts a run of original promo
videos to his own X account, crediting help from [[hokus_pokus]]:

> "Gs, I made a promo edit." / "Here goes nothing. I'll send two vids." /
> "It's called rain dancing" / "Yessir. Had some help along the way
> @Hokus_Pokus20"
> — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) (🔥×5, then 🫡×2–3
> across the thread)

The "raindancing" coinage itself predates this by about a week — it enters
the chat as a slash-chant on [28 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md), see [[community-culture]] —
but Daniel R is one of its most persistent evangelists, titling multiple
videos around it and posting a second and third round in the following
days ("I made out another vid for the lols" — [5 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md); "outworked? My ass. Here's a
new vid" — [10 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-10.md)).

## The breadcrumb theorist

Daniel R is also one of the chat's more elaborate amateur numerologists,
building multi-step timing theories out of [[fluxcapacitor]] quotes and
terminal post imagery — a habit that persists well past the launch window,
long after most people's ticker-hunt theorizing (see
[[launch-and-origins]]) had stopped:

> "A few days ago, Terminal tweeted something about a pump on Saturday
> night. Later, Tate tweeted a post of him :) with the headlines 'when bro

> had FluxCapicitor say 'We need 79 hell fucking yeahs'. What's 79 days
> after the day this coin was created(Oct 24)? January,11, Saturday."
> — [4 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-04.md) (❤×3,👍×1)

> "Flux said we missed breadcrumbs. Well this pic has breadcrumbs and
> theres 3 baguettes. Wonder what that could mean?🧐"
> — [10 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-10.md)

## The long-haul fixture

From roughly February 2025 onward, Daniel R's posting settles almost
entirely into the "Gm :)" / "GM:)" ritual documented on
[[smiley-heartbeat]] — several times a week, for over a year straight,
with reaction counts climbing over time (single digits in early 2025,
routinely 5–10 🔥 by late 2025/2026 — a genuinely well-liked, recognized
fixture rather than background noise). Real personal life surfaces inside
that ritual rather than displacing it: a trip that puts him "Currently in
Dominican Republic" ([5 Aug
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-05.md)), an open breakup ("Hey Gs
going through a breakup rn shit sucks" — [26 Aug
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-26.md), ❤×1,🕊×1) that he processes in
the chat over the following weeks, and taking up boxing as a result ("had
my first spar today Gs went great:)stay positive 🙏" — [28 Sep
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-09/2025-09-28.md), ❤×6).

On the coin's one-year anniversary he breaks from the daily ritual for a
genuine retrospective:

> "Happy birthday $TATEAI:) Thank you brothers for all the support and
> advice this past year you guys are genuinely amazing and i'm so very
> grateful I have found this fucking amazing community. I'm not very
> active here but just know I ain't slacking and I'm bettering myself
> everyday:)"
> — [24 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-24.md) (🤝×6,❤×2)
