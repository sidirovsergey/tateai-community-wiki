# G (always lucky 🍀)

Among this wiki's documented entities, G is the second-highest-volume poster
after [[rgk1]] — but with a completely different shape to the activity:
where rgk1 sustained an in-character voice for months, roughly 96% of G's ~4,474
messages land inside a single ten-week window — the launch rush from
[28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md) through
[2 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-02.md). After that he all but
disappears, surfacing only in occasional one-off check-ins through
[26 Jun 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-06/2025-06-26.md), his last dated message
in this archive.

## The launch window: five nights with no sleep

G's very first messages, within an hour of his first appearance, are him
asking admins to unban another member — a small early signal he already had
some standing in the room. From there he becomes one of the loudest voices
in the chat's founding "which ticker is the real one" scavenger hunt (see
[[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] for that lore), posting in the hundreds
per day and openly documenting his own sleep deprivation and financial
risk-taking in real time:

> "Lol I understand you want followers bro and be able to say I called this
> but just stop it's like this is the one play no call group needs to call
> or no one person needs to shill like crazy for. This is literally coded
> for 9 figures+. Let the people figure it out for themselves"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)

> "One day I thought I was him the next I was drinking myself into a coma on
> the brink of suicide. Those were some fun days. Happy to be back tho and
> alive still pushing and making things work. NEVER STOP CLICKING BOYS!!!!!"
> — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

> "I just maxed out my credit card lol got $700 more ready to load if it
> gives me a mill touch"
> — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

That night he also describes his parents handing him $1,000 to invest on
their behalf and admitting he's tempted to "full port" it into the same
coin, alongside a self-narrated trading history: a former AMC/GME retail
trader who says he round-tripped most of an AMC position but made close to
$100k on GME, which let him "do futures full time." The tone throughout is
sincere hype rather than performance — rally cries ("full port," "patience,"
"we truly have the ticker," "dipmond hands"), constant reassurance to
newer/shakier holders, and running personal commentary (fasting, no-fap,
psychedelics, politics) mixed in with the market calls.

## Sustained conviction into early 2025

G stays highly active through November and December 2024 and into the first
days of January 2025, cycling through the same "hold, don't raid, the
evidence is overwhelming" talking points as the coin's price chopped
sideways, along with the chat-wide "rain dance" refrain (he uses it as early
as [10 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-10.md), around the same
period [[widelton]] adopts it as a personal catchphrase):

> "500m minimum or nothing"
> — [5 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-05.md), 🔥×6

## Sporadic returns

After early January 2025 his presence drops off sharply — the remaining
~170 messages in the archive (about 4% of his total) are spread thin across
six months, each one a brief "long time, checking back in" reappearance
rather than sustained participation:

> "Btw hello gs. Long time... Lol saw this and had to come check what was
> up in here. Still holding all my coins. How long has it been lol? Talk
> about a test of faith and patience."
> — [19 May 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-05/2025-05-19.md)

Nothing in the sampled material explains the "always lucky 🍀" display name
itself — it isn't referenced or joked about by G or anyone else in the
material read for this page, so this wiki isn't going to invent an origin
for it.
