# Ronald

A launch-week raid voice whose defining trait is volume and repetition
rather than any one bit: hundreds of one-line chants, slash-command spam,
and diamond-hands affirmations, occasionally interrupted by real personal
disclosure. First message lands
[31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md), the day after the
group's founding, and Ronald never really leaves — activity just gets
sparser and sparser, down to isolated bare `:)` messages by mid-2025 and a
final scattered handful through
[9 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-09.md).

## The bit: personally acquainted with Andrew Tate

On his first active night, Ronald runs a short extended joke claiming a
direct personal relationship with Andrew Tate:

> "Tate is my good friend he said that he will be pushing this soon" /
> "He's on the phone to me" / "I got him that hair piece for Easter" /
> "He loves it" / "He says he loves us and he will be seeing us soon👨🏽‍🦲🤖"
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

Nothing in the surrounding messages suggests anyone took this literally —
it reads as an in-the-moment riff rather than a claim Ronald expected to be
believed.

## Slash-chant spam, a recurring format for years

Like many launch-era posters (see [[community-culture]]), Ronald fires the
same all-caps slash-tag five to twenty times in a row as a group chant:
`/TATEAI_IS_STRONG`, `/NIGGA_AI`, `/WE_AINT_FUCKING_LEAVING`,
`/BuyTATEAIorStayBROKE`, `/HELL_FUCKING_YEAH`, `/RAIN_DANCE`,
`/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`, and `/FIRST_ONE_TO_SELL_IS_GAY` (immediately
followed, seventeen messages of the same chant later, by his own correction
`/FIRST_ONE_TO_SELL_IS_STRAIGHT` — [18 Jan
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-18.md)). The signature closing emoji
across late 2024/early 2025 is 💎🖐🏿, later joined by 👁️🫦👁️ as a second
running tic.

## The TRW ban

A rare moment of genuine (rather than performed) frustration comes the same
first night, after Ronald gets banned from Andrew Tate's paid community
The Real World for talking about TateAI there:

> "I can't believe it I've been banned from the TRW / For talkin about
> Tateai" ... "I've just lost all my power level" ... "But I'm actually
> distraught bro I love TRW / I've spent so long in there I've just lost
> all my PL"
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

## Real financial stakes

Underneath the hype-chant surface, Ronald periodically discloses real
money pressure — small bags, real consequences:

> "Sorry guys I had to sell 15 dollars worth to pay for food"
> — [15 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-15.md)

> "Yeah I've been off work for 3 weeks ): / No more moneys"
> — [2 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-02.md)

## A harsher pattern worth flagging plainly

On the same first night as the jokes above, Ronald also posts an extended
antisemitic, Holocaust-denial rant unprompted by anything in the
surrounding conversation — *"u can holocaust is a fucken hoax, hilter was a
G for the most part he had good ideas his people loved him"* — drawing 🔥×2
💯×2 in reaction, followed minutes later by more generalized claims about
Jewish people controlling "banks, media, government"
([31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)). Per this wiki's
honesty standard (see [[igelo]] for a comparable case), this is worth
naming directly rather than folding into "colorful launch-era chat" — it's
a specific, sustained ideological claim, not generic edgy slang, and it
drew visible approval at the time.

## Theories and community fixtures

Ronald engages seriously with the chat's running mysteries — floating that
[[fluxcapacitor]] might literally be Andrew Tate (*"Flux capacitor is
Andrew"*, [3 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-03.md)) — and
repeatedly credits Widelton and the "rain dance" ethos (see
[[widelton]], [[diamond-hands]]) as the chat's operating philosophy through
early 2025.

## Fade-out

After Feb 2025 Ronald's presence thins to occasional bare `:)` messages and
month-plus gaps, the same pattern seen across most of this chat's launch-era
voices. The last dated message in this material is an unexplained fragment:

> "not if you want to be RichMillionaire"
> — [9 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-09.md)
