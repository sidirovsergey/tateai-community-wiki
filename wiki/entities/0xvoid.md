# 0xVoid ✈️

The sharpest degen-trader voice among this chat's launch-week regulars —
articulate, leverage-obsessed, and the one who put the community's
diamond-hands rhetoric into its clearest words. Like the other high-volume
launch-era figures on this wiki, 0xVoid's entire documented presence (1,737
messages) sits in a short, front-loaded window: [27 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) to [6 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-06.md) — the narrowest span of the four
covered here, barely ten days, all in the first week and a half after
launch.

## Hedge-everything trading style

0xVoid's opening messages set the tone: playing all three Tate-adjacent
coins at once as an explicit hedge, not a conviction bet — *"Honestly I’m
playing all 3 in case 🤣"* — [27 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md). Recurring threads:

- Detailed position-tracking: entries, re-entries, leverage sizing, and P&L
  reported in SOL, e.g. *"Took a -12 sol loss on $TATE but am up like +115
  on $TateAI"* — [29 Oct
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md).
- Genuine on-chain investigation — dev wallet tracing, testing whether
  CEX-to-token transfers are traceable on Solscan — in the same register as
  [[hokus_pokus]].
- Informal moderation: asks for ban/mute perms multiple times, personally
  de-escalates conflicts ("Spyro is a cool dude he just tryna make everyone
  money while we wait. We can tell him to do it in a different chat not
  here."), and issues soft threats to spammers ("You have 1 min b4 ban if
  not bot 🤣").

## The diamond-hands manifesto

This is the source of a quote worth verifying carefully, since it's easy to
flatten into something more generic than what was actually said. On [29 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md), 0xVoid writes a multi-message
argument for why this community's version of diamond-handing is different
from the earlier `$RNT`/`$DADDY` cycles (see [[diamond-hands]]):

> "He wants people this time to put in the work because the communities who
> are strong are those who put in the WORK. Leads to a more cult vibe and
> more diamond hands than people just riding off Andrew's pump, and jeeting
> and leaving."

> "This time is different, I'm putting in the work, actually taking the time
> to understand wtf is going on, and I'm definitely going to be diamond
> handing this even more because of that and this community we have is too
> retarded. Yall retards got me going schizo spamming penis reduction, and
> therefore I am part of the cult and will diamond hand my bags."

The "diamond handing this even more because of the community" framing does
hold up as a real, sincere-sounding statement — it's just embedded in a much
longer, funnier, more self-aware riff than the phrase alone suggests.

## The "penis reduction" raid

On the morning of [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md),
0xVoid is a central instigator of one of the chat's most absurd coordinated
bits: dozens of members, 0xVoid heavily among them, spam the phrase "penis
reduction" for over an hour on the theory that the `@tate_terminal` bot (see
[[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]]) was reading the chat and could be baited
into tweeting it — complete with slash-command chants
(`/PENIS_REDUCTION_TATEAI`) and real-time theorizing about the bot's tweet
queue. It's presented as pure schizo-posting for its own sake, not a serious
theory: *"i am turning schizo... i have been schizo since day 1."*

A separate, calmer catchphrase — *"preparations have been met"* — recurs
through early November as a dry, deadpan response to good news, e.g. [2 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md).
