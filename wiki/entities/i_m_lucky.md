# I’m lucky

One of the loudest true-believer voices of launch week, and — like
[[limalemon]] and [[orchestra_lima_maxi]] — a name that turns out to be a
genuine self-description rather than an empty handle: on a rare 2025
return visit he explicitly cashes in the pun himself (see below). The
overwhelming majority of the archive — roughly 854 of 954 messages — lands
in a single fortnight, [30 Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)–[12
Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md), after which the account
goes almost silent, surfacing only for occasional nostalgic check-ins
through [18 Jan 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-18.md).

## The ticker theorist

I’m lucky is one of the chat's most prolific contributors to the founding
"which coin is real" mythology documented in [[launch-and-origins]] —
reading Andrew Tate's EM quotes ("some of you have found it," "only the
pure will survive the fire") as deliberate clues pointing at `$TateAI`
over the rival `$TATE`, and treating the terminal's refusal to post a
contract address as confirmation rather than absence of evidence:

> "If it was $tate why hasn't the CA been connected to the terminal"
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md) (❤×2,🔥×1,💯×1)

> "This is Issa (the creator of $TATE) dm with Andrew Tate at the start of
> all this shit. Why would Tate ask the creator of TATE if he wants the
> ticker??? Think"
> — [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md) (👍×1)

The chat's central "we are a cult" chant (see [[community-culture]]) counts
I’m lucky among its participants — two of the seven senders posting the
literal string `/WE_ARE_A_CULT` in the same two-minute window on [31 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md) — alongside a stream of similar
one-line raid chants that night: `/WE_AINT_FUCKING_LEAVING`,
`/IS_PAM_ALIVE`, `/HUMAN_VS_MACHINE_TATE_VS_TATEAI`,
`/BIG_DICK_VS_ERECTILE_DYSFUNCTION`. He also briefly adopts the 🧘
"meditating" de-escalation bit that [[hokus_pokus]]'s page documents as
that user's signature tic, in the same first week — not unique to either
of them, evidently a shared community reflex in that window rather than
one person's invention.

## The verified quote

This chat's most-cited line elsewhere in this wiki's source material checks
out against the raw log. In full, with the context usually trimmed off it:

> "We really are like an army of robots. I think Tates experiment actually
> worked and the AI created the craziest community 😂"
> — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) (👍×2)

It sits inside a longer riff that day about turning the community into an
organized "robot army" of raiders and AI bots — including one of the
earliest uses of "raindancing" as a verb, quoted in full on
[[community-culture]]: *"With enough AIs working for us we will be the
very first community that is raindancing 24/7!! DO YOU UNDERSTAND?"*

## Gatekeeper, never officially admin

Unlike [[limalemon]], who explicitly becomes admin, I’m lucky never claims
the title himself but repeatedly acts as an informal gatekeeper — leading
a sustained campaign against making a specific user ("Chadnaldo") admin,
citing his history of shilling a rival coin:

> "Do not make chadnaldo admin he's not trust worthy" / "Chadnaldo was
> fudding us and shilling his shitcoin 3 days ago" / "Once a fudder always
> a fudder. Trust lost"
> — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md) (❤×2)

The launch-week voice is also, bluntly, one of the archive's heavier users
of slurs — "faggot," "retard," and occasional racial slurs appear
constantly in his posts from this period, consistent with (not unusual
against) the chat's baseline register that week. Flagged plainly rather
than smoothed over, per this wiki's honesty standard.

## The long tail: an OG who checks back in

After 12 Nov 2024 the account goes almost dark, returning in short bursts
months and sometimes a year apart — each time reading as genuine nostalgia
rather than renewed hype. On a January 2025 return he plays on his own
name directly:

> "Thanks bro yea it's been chaos on the market so I've been busy, but I’m
> lucky 😂 we're all gonna make it. Missed you guys too hope everyone's
> surviving the trenches it's hard going rn"
> — [13 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-13.md) (❤×1)

By mid-2025 the tone has settled into something closer to reflective
affection than conviction:

> "This was the most fun thing I ever experienced in crypto. The original
> gathering of clues, connecting the dots. Now this place feels like home,
> I always check back here. Big love for all the OGs ❤️"
> — [17 Jul 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-07/2025-07-17.md) (❤×6)

His last logged message in this dataset is a check-in seven months later:
"How we doing boys?" — [18 Jan
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-18.md) (❤×8).
