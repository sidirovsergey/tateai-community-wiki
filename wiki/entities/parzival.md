# **PARZIVAL**

The display name carries its asterisks into the chat itself — Telegram
renders it as bold, `**PARZIVAL**`, in every single one of their 911
messages, so it's kept here as part of the name rather than stripped as
markdown noise. Like [[dom]] (13 days) and 94% of
[[orchestra_lima_maxi]]'s volume (ten weeks), PARZIVAL's entire presence in
the archive is confined to a single dense launch-era window: [27 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) through [22 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-22.md) — under four weeks — and then
nothing. No later reappearance was found anywhere in the sampled material.

surrounding messages appears anywhere in the sampled material — flagged
