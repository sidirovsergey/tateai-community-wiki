# Limalemon

The second-highest-volume poster in the entire 165k-message archive — behind
only "Deleted Account," which is not a person (see
[[deleted-accounts]]). That makes Limalemon, in practical terms, the single
most prolific *individual* voice in this chat's history. Referred to
elsewhere in the chat by handle as `@LimaLemon777` — both [[cloud_fortune]]
and [[orchestra_lima_maxi]] mention him by that handle as a well-known top
holder ("Lima is Tate's Insider"). Note the coincidence: Orchestra's own
display name also happens to contain "Lima" — a different person, disambiguated
on that page. Roughly half of
that lifetime output — 5,560 of 11,045 messages — landed in a single month:
November 2024, the launch month. What follows is a genuine arc, not just
volume: chaotic self-appointed launch-night whale, then admin/moderator,
then a long-haul holder who stuck around long enough to turn openly bitter
about the wider Tate-branded coin ecosystem before settling into the same
`:)`-only lurker rhythm as everyone else still standing by 2026.

## Launch night: the self-styled whale (2–3 Nov 2024)

Limalemon's first messages, clocked at [2 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md), are not analysis or hype in the
rgk1 mold (see [[rgk1]]) — they're a one-man chaos performance, claiming
personal responsibility for every pump and dump in the chart, threatening to
"test" the community's resolve, and addressing the room almost entirely in
slur-laced Solana-degen slang (consistent with this chat's baseline register
in its first days — see the raw log itself, where even `Deleted Account`
messages use the same words). A representative burst from that first night:

> "Next 10-30 minutes il dump 30M coins to the market have fun surviving
> niggas hahahhaa"
> — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)

He self-declares as an out-of-control market mover ("Im the biggwst whsle
never forget," "Im crazy and unpreditable") and, within the same hour, as
the chat's most-moderated user ("Dude im the most banned user in this
group"). By the next morning he's shifted from chaos-whale bit into
something closer to a rallying cry, posting one of his only fully
coherent, deliberately-composed messages of the whole archive — an all-caps
mission statement aimed at new members questioning whether TateAI was
really "the ticker":

> "DONT WORRY ABOUT IF WE ARE THE TICKER.. I CAN TELL YOU THIS COMMUNITY IS
> BIGGER THAN BEEING A TICKER! [...] WE NEED TO REACH 2 MILLION MARKETCAP AND
> A LOT OF VOLUME. USE AI BOTS USE SOCIAL MEDIA USE CONTACTS. USE EVERYTHING
> THATS IN YOUR POWER AND BRING IDEAS. [...] SO START TO FUCKING WORK FOR YOUR
> BAGS MY DIAMOND BALL NIGGERS!!!!!"
> — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) (❤×2, 🔥×1, 🫡×1)

## Becoming admin

Later that same day, after repeatedly lobbying for it ("Can someone make me
admin?", "Get me admin rights in the next 8 hours"), it happens:

> "Im Admin now Niggad"
> — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) (🔥×3)

He immediately starts acting the part — banning spam/scam accounts ("I
banned some people for beeing scammers and promoting shitcoins in the
group"), fielding "which admin restricted @X" questions from other members
for months afterward, and deliberately deferring the technical on-chain
work to others ("Thats our chart guy trust him not me" — he is the
hype/leadership figure, not the analyst; contrast [[sergei]]'s
gatekeeping-analyst role in the same period). He keeps a moderator or
admin-adjacent presence deep into the chat's life — a bare `/ban` shows up
as late as [1 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-01.md).

## The long-haul holder

Where the launch-night bit was pure theater, Limalemon's actual trading
behavior — as far as it's traceable in his own words — is straightforward
diamond-handing. By the end of 2024 he's telling sellers off in exactly the
terms rgk1 uses (see [[diamond-hands]]):

> "I hold exactly the amount i had in the beginning"
> — [24 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-24.md)

His monthly volume tells its own story of the chat's broader decay after
launch: 5,560 messages in Nov 2024 alone, dropping to 372 in Dec 2024, a
brief second wave of 1,147 in Jan 2025, then a long decline — 272 in June
2025, down to single digits (10) by Dec 2025.

## January 2026: the disillusionment

That decline breaks for one last spike — 473 messages in January 2026 —
and a big chunk of it is a sustained, uncharacteristically reflective rant
against Andrew Tate and the broader "Tate" coin ecosystem (not this
project specifically; see [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] for why those
are different things). He states concrete personal losses and lands on a
harsh verdict:

> "Bro i lost already 30k just on tateai." / "And 20k on other tate
> products." [...] "Typical scammer."
> — [29 Jan 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-29.md)

The same stretch includes remarks about Tate's team and associates that
cross into antisemitic conspiracy territory (not reproduced here) — worth
flagging plainly rather than sanitizing away, per this wiki's honesty
standard, but not the load-bearing part of the rant. The actual point he
keeps circling back to is more interesting: he explicitly separates his
anger at Tate from his position in *this* coin —

> "The only good coin he made ist tateai 50k liquidity 100k mc" / "And its
> not because of him its because of us because no one sold"
> — [29 Jan 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-29.md)

— and directly criticizes the chat's own low-effort culture, taking aim at
the very meme this wiki documents elsewhere (see [[smiley-heartbeat]]):

> "Im not crying. Im telling the group that its not enough to say :)
> anymore"
> — [29 Jan 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-29.md)

He spends the rest of that message run pitching concrete project ideas (an
AI-agent job marketplace paid in $TateAI, fee buybacks) rather than just
venting — the same instinct shows up again in April/May 2026, still
pitching similar ideas about a "terminator hologram" and an agent job
board, and still explicitly holding: "and as i told u im still holding my
tateai."

## Settling into the heartbeat

By mid-2026 Limalemon's cadence collapses into the same pattern documented
on [[smiley-heartbeat]] — "Gm :)", bare "🔥"-reaction-drawing ":)"s, long
gaps between appearances. His last logged message in this dataset is a bare
`:)` on [10 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-10.md).
