# Community culture

This page covers the chat's general tone, in-jokes, and recurring bits that
don't already have their own page. It does **not** re-explain the bare `:)`
heartbeat (see [[smiley-heartbeat]]), [[fluxcapacitor]], or the ticker/CA
hunt that founded the chat (see [[launch-and-origins]]) — go there for
those. What follows is everything else: the FUD/shill vocabulary that is
the launch era's dominant background noise, the community's own explicit
"we are a cult" framing, "raindancing" as a term of art, and the chat's
low-grade, mostly-unfounded rug paranoia.

## FUD and shill: the launch era's love language

In the first three weeks (26 Oct – ~18 Nov 2024, per [[timeline]] the
period holding roughly 70% of the archive's entire message volume),
"fud" and "shill" are the two most reflexive words in the chat — used
constantly, self-consciously, and mostly as a running joke about
themselves rather than a real accusation:

> "Ehm i have to gO fUd oThEr CoinS so my 10 ruPeeS in $tate thAt i BouGht
> aT 25M turn iNto 20 RuPees"
> — Deleted Account, [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)

> "Sorry I need to fud 😂 to much addiction"
> — IGelo, [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)

> "GM… the chat got a bit fuddy yesterday with the 'I'm selling' joke 🤦🏽‍♂️🙄
> let's get back on track."
> — Ekul🫡, [13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md)

[[sergei]] has a habitual hedge that recurs across the whole archive
whenever he raises a genuinely critical point — "not fud, just question":

> "Why we think $TateAI will pump? […] Nobody promised this to us. Pump
> require huge buys, and we all already bought our bags. Not FUD, just
> question to find reasonable answers."
> — Sergei, [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)

"Raiding" — coordinated posting/replying on X/Twitter to promote the
ticker — is treated with the same mix of sincerity and self-awareness:

> "Is what I am doing pointless? Maybe but it's more about the energy, the
> commitment and the belief […] This one today I clocked up 300 raids in
> two days."
> — Orchestra | Lima MAXI, [14 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-14.md)

## "We are a cult" — accusation and badge of pride, at the same time

The community calls itself a cult constantly, starting on the chat's very
first day, and the framing is never quite settled between joke, warning,
and genuine pride:

> "you guys realize he's creating a cult here?"
> — God Candle, [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)

> "Hello losers welcome to our cult. You might be here early but we are
> not the same."
> — ., [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)

The community also borrows outside legitimacy for the label, citing the
crypto trader Murad's "cult coins" theory as validation:

> "I understand what Murad meant when he said that the cults are the ones
> sustaining the charts by buying the dips haha. Guess I'm part of the cult
> now"
> — Rari, [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

Minutes later, at least seven different senders (`Deleted Account`,
Centurion ×2, `I'm lucky` ×2, Rari, Gem Hunter, HIM LOKO) post the exact
literal string `/WE_ARE_A_CULT` in a two-minute window
([31 Oct 2024, 02:17–02:44](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)) — a
slash-command-formatted group chant, not organic conversation. The same
chant format recurs with `/WE_ARE_THE_ONLY_COMMUNITY_RAINDANCING` (Cloud
Fortune ×3, IGelo ×1, within one minute,
[28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)) and
`/LUCKY_DIDNT_FUD` (three identical posts,
[30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)). Whatever the source
of these — a shared script, an admin instruction to copy-paste, a raid
tactic imported from X — the chat repeatedly organizes itself around
literal identical-text chanting, and "cult" is the label it applies to
itself for doing so, approvingly: Cloud Fortune referring to Murad's
"real diamond hands mindset" tweet months later
([21 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-21.md)) shows the Murad
reference point sticking around well past the launch window. By 2025 the
framing has cooled into a running joke rather than a live debate — "Guys I
found the Mexican $tate cult" (Gabriel,
[7 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-07.md)).

## Raindancing

"Raindancing" is this community's own coinage for visible, repeated,
public displays of devotion to the project — raiding, shilling, or just
loudly declaring loyalty — framed as a performance aimed specifically at
getting Andrew Tate's attention. It enters the chat as the slash-chant
covered above (`/WE_ARE_THE_ONLY_COMMUNITY_RAINDANCING`,
[28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)) and within days
becomes an ordinary verb:

> "With enough AIs working for us we will be the very first community that
> is raindancing 24/7!! DO YOU UNDERSTAND?"
> — I'm lucky, [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md)

> "And always tag andrew. He wants you to let him know you are
> raindancing. He said this many times before"
> — Deleted Account, [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md)

Unlike most launch-era vocabulary, "raindancing" doesn't die with the rest
of the hype — it resurfaces sporadically for well over a year, long after
the chat has settled into its quiet baseline: Sergei uses it in passing
("Terminal raindancing) silly cloudfucker)",
[7 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-07.md)), Gabriel uses it
literally ("Raindancing with some weights now",
[26 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-26.md)), and it's still in
use in mid-2025 ("60 years old and he is raindancing with us" — sbzgh,
[8 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-08.md)).

## WAGMI, and a much thinner "GM TateAI"

"WAGMI" ("we're all gonna make it") is the chat's generic ambient
affirmation, present from the first days
([28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)) straight through to
2025 with no real evolution — it means what it means anywhere in
crypto-Telegram and doesn't develop community-specific inflection the way
"raindancing" or "diamond hands" (see [[diamond-hands]]) do.

Worth flagging honestly: "GM TateAI" reads at first like it could be a
parallel running bit, but the raw material only supports four instances,
all from [[rgk1]] specifically (31 Dec 2024, 11 Jan, 17 Jan, 12 Feb 2025) —
this is a personal habit of one prolific poster, not a chat-wide morning
ritual. Don't overstate it.

## Background rug paranoia (not one incident)

"Rug" and its variants appear roughly 566 times across 117 different day
logs — heavily concentrated in the Oct–Nov 2024 launch window (the single
day 29 Oct 2024 alone has 34 matches) and tapering to occasional use for
the rest of the archive. Reading a sample confirms the task brief's
suspicion: this is diffuse, generic paranoia and dark humor about
memecoins in general, not an account of one specific event that happened to
this project.

> "If it's not it's the most well thought out rug of all fucking time"
> — G (always lucky 🍀), [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

> "My biggest round trip was holding through the Floki rugs back in 2021 😂
> crazy times"
> — •CrypticBeachBum•🌴, [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

One chat member's entire display name is **Rugerman** — mostly posting
gifs and terse hold-encouragement ("Hold mfers hold.", "Hold Brothers",
[29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)) — the community's
biggest fear worn as a username and repurposed as a rallying cry, which
fits the chat's general habit of defusing anxiety with a joke.

The one specific, recurring, genuinely citable rug reference is **not**
about this community's own `$TateAI` — it's "the tate rug," shorthand for
Andrew Tate's own `$Tate`/`$DADDY` coin cratering, cited well over a year
later as retroactive vindication for having picked `$TateAI` instead:

> "100% insider, predicted the tate rug, and the terminal replied to us
> when we used the word flux capacitor"
> — sbzgh, [5 Mar 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-03/2026-03-05.md)

> "fairrr, Tate rug we all predicted but the reply thing is enough! Ty"
> — orez, [5 Mar 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-03/2026-03-05.md)

No message in the sampled material describes `$TateAI` itself as having
been rugged. The honest summary is: constant low-grade rug anxiety as
background texture (mostly jokes, mostly about crypto in general), plus
one specific, real belief the community holds about a *different* coin
(Tate's own) that it treats as having validated its own choice.
