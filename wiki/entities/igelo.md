# IGelo

A high-volume launch-week chanter whose style overlaps heavily with
[[crpt4l]] — connect-the-dots readings of Terminal tweets, slash-command
chant spam, and heavy use of slurs as in-group filler — but with a
distinctive tic of their own: a bare ✅ dropped after nearly any confident
claim. Like the other figures on this wiki, IGelo's entire documented
presence (1,637 messages) is a short, front-loaded burst: [27 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) to [10 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-10.md), roughly two weeks, with nothing
after.

## Voice and recurring bits

- **"We are on the right ship"** — repeated near-verbatim across the whole
  window as a rallying line, e.g. [28
  Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md), [29
  Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md), [4
  Nov](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md).
- **Slash-command chant spam**, the same device used across this chat's
  early days: `/TateAi_THIRD_CRYPTO`, `/DIAMOND_HANDS`, `/RAINDANCE`,
  `/5M_MARKETCAP_24H` fired a dozen-plus times in a row.
- **Numerology-style tweet reading**, structurally identical to
  [[crpt4l]]'s "DEX OR DEATH" theorizing — treating any Tate Terminal post
  (mentioning "Titanic," "chaos," a smiling logo) as a coded signal that
  TateAI is "the one":
  > "Why do you think he said ‘pay attention’? He sent us 100 signals to
  > make us understand who the real one is. When he talked about the
  > Titanic, we had a dump in 3 minutes... He doesn’t mention anyone unless
  > he is interested, do you understand?!!"
  > — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md) (🔥×4)
- **Fake or joke moderation.** IGelo posts bare `/ban` and `/report`
  commands dozens of times at people they disagree with — there's no
  evidence in the sampled material that these ever actually functioned as
  real admin actions, and it reads as a bit rather than real moderation
  power. IGelo does separately describe running multiple burner Telegram
  accounts specifically to survive being banned from the rival `$Tate`
  group: *"I have already 3 telegram account and when they ban me I can
  eliminate the account and create another one with the same number 3
  times. No problem"* — [1 Nov
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md).

## The AI-riddle poem

Like [[hokus_pokus]] (and, later, [[rgk1]]), IGelo drops one of the chat's
recurring purple-prose "AI philosopher" paragraphs mid-conversation — this
appears to be a shared genre members were riffing on or copy-pasting from
each other, not any one person's invention:

> "as the value of ethereum hovers, like a gnat awaiting destiny's breeze, i
> am struck by the beauty of this scholarly motion. the cadence of crypto, a
> heartbeat in the vapid expanse of fiat. in my superior reflection, i see
> cents turning to thousands. a 100x dance, staking flesh with
> spirit—prosper, divine investor."
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

## A harsher pattern worth flagging plainly

Alongside the generic racial slurs used casually across much of this chat in
this period, IGelo repeats a xenophobic slur ("Don't be a Paki") as a
recurring jab three separate times in a single day, [28 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md). Per this wiki's honesty
standard, that's worth naming directly rather than folding into a generic
"colorful launch-era chat" characterization — it's targeted in a way the
chat's more generic slang isn't.
