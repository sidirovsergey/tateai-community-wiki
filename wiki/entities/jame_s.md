# Jame S

The chat's self-appointed morale officer and raid captain. One of the
highest-volume posters in the whole archive, and — unlike most of the other
launch-era heavy hitters (compare [[arthur]], who vanishes entirely after
Dec 2024) — still posting a daily "GM :)" as late as [8 Mar
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-03/2026-03-08.md). Self-identifies as Australian ([4 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md): *"I live in Australia and I
still feel like I need Trump to win haha"*) and 18 years old ([5 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md): *"I'm 18 too bro, if we hold for
that 100x our lives are changed"*), and later marks turning 20 on [29 Jan
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-29.md) — so he was posting through this
chat's entire arc from age 18 to 20.

## The raid captain (Oct–Nov 2024)

Jame S's defining early behavior is organizing and commanding the chat's
Twitter/X "raid" campaigns — coordinated reply-brigading of the parent
terminal's posts to push `$TateAi` visibility. He posts slash-command raid
calls in repeated bursts (`/RAID_TIME`, `/irrationally_positive`,
`/HELL_FUCKING_YEAH`, `/The_ones_who_dont_raid_are_GAY`) and follows them
with genuine tactical instructions:

> "Quick word lads, some of our tweets I've noticed are being hidden as
> 'probable spam'... If you don't want this to happen to your reply then
> you're gonna have do more than just say $TateAi and add a smiley face...
> Let's try to make our replies as captivating and human as possible"
> — [14 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-14.md)

He pairs this with a self-improvement, MMA-training register that recurs
constantly:

> "I'm off to train MMA boys, stay on it and don't slack on your training
> either. If nothing else do your pushups for the karmic retribution. And
> just the gains too."
> — [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)

And with long, earnest morale addresses to the room, the closest thing this
chat has to a designated hype-speech-giver:

> "It's time to act. We should all be consistently making replies on the
> terminals tweets and posting these photos along with other evidence you
> have to spread our message... With or without the terminals help. That
> should be our mindset, we need each other to make this work."
> — [8 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-08.md) (❤×13)

## Long-haul holder (2025–2026)

Once the launch rush fades, Jame S's volume drops sharply but never stops —
he becomes one of the chat's steady daily-check-in regulars, a near-ritual
"GM :)" or "GM boys" appearing several times a week for years, still getting
reliable double-digit 🔥 reactions in late 2025 and early 2026. The
all-or-nothing conviction voice survives intact:

> "Do you believe in Tates coins? Yes or fucking no. / I like all-or-nothing
> types of men. Men like me. Men who would rather go broke than act without
> balls in the first place. Skin in the game."
> — [5 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-05.md) (🔥×5)

> "This year I enter my 20s, I know these years will be the some of the most
> important, dialled and for-filling years of my life, with or without any
> crypto pumps. But we holding never the less 👊"
> — [29 Jan 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-01/2026-01-29.md)
