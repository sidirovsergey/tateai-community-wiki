# Crpt4l

A Norway-based early buyer whose entire documented presence in this chat is a
single, ferociously dense eight-week burst at the very start of the
community's life. Every one of Crpt4l's 1,798 messages falls between [27 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) (05:36, hours into the "which
coin is real" scavenger hunt — see [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] /
[[launch-and-origins]]) and [19 Dec
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-19.md), with activity already thinning
sharply after [22 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-22.md) and only
three throwaway messages ("Woooo", "Lfg Guys", "Woooo") on that final date.
There is no trace of Crpt4l in later eras of the chat. Whether that means
they left, went silent, or simply aren't in the material sampled for this
page isn't determinable from chat content alone — but within the window
covered here, the pattern is unambiguous: this is a launch-week phenomenon,
not a long-run participant.

## Voice

Crpt4l posts in short, all-caps-heavy bursts, often a dozen one-line messages
in a row, built around a handful of repeated moves:

- **The OG claim.** From the first hours, Crpt4l repeats a self-identifying
  origin story — bought in at "500k mc," reasserted constantly as proof of
  early conviction: *"I’m here since 500k mc 7 days ago just woke up we we
  pump lfg"* — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md).
- **"WOOOO" / "LFG."** The single most repeated unit in the corpus — often
  posted alone, sometimes stacked five and six times in a minute.
- **Diamond-hands theorizing**, tightly on-brand for the chat's dominant
  narrative (see [[diamond-hands]]):
  > "Andrew talk a lot about diamond hands and fuck jeet noobs, one day he
  > gonna send this TateAI and make the true diamond hand believers rich
  > rich, that’s my theory"
  > — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) (🔥×3, ❤×1)
- **Family-motivated bag-holding.** A recurring, sincere-sounding refrain
  distinct from the chat's usual money-porn tone:
  > "Gonna buy my mom a car pay of debt family lfg"
  > — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)
- **Late-night tangents**, usually in the 02:00–07:00 Norway-time window,
  sliding from token talk into DMT/lucid-dreaming/Annunaki/Freemason
  conspiracy riffing with whoever else is still awake, then snapping back to
  "TATEAI TO THE MOON."

## The rival-chat troll and ban

On [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md) Crpt4l describes
running a second account inside the rival `$TATE` group specifically to
argue that TateAI, not Tate, was the "real" coin — and on [3 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) reports getting banned for it:

> "I said so much shit in the Tate tg and didn’t get banned, but telling them
> to buy the real one TateAI and I’m GONE😭"

This cross-raiding of the rival `$TATE` telegram is not unique to Crpt4l —
see [[igelo]] for the same pattern — and appears to have been a recognized
community activity in this window, not one person's private stunt.
