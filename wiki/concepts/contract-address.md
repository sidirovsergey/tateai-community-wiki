# The Contract Address (CA)

`$TateAI`'s Solana contract address is `BoBj68cWnCvzMNUKzJyR7Jq7tLM3v76D1pYL1E8rpump` —
recorded today in the bot repo's own `config/bootstrap-config.json`, and by a wide
margin the single most-repeated string in this chat's entire history (762
keyword matches for "contract address"/"ca" alone, before counting the bare
pastes of the address itself). The story worth telling isn't really "what is
the CA" — it's that this community spent its entire founding week (and,
periodically, years afterward) trying to get an *official* answer to that
question and never quite got one.

## It was in the chat before anyone could prove it

The address itself shows up within the first few hours of the export. People
were asking for it before anyone had it — `Larry Doran: Send the ca adress`
and `Yous: Ca ????` / `/ca` both land inside the chat's first ten minutes,
[26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md) — but nobody posts the
actual string until **Cyph's** long "why I bought TateAI" thesis post at
06:07 that morning, followed by **God Candle** pasting it bare at 13:38 the
same day. Cyph's post is effectively the CA's founding document in this chat:
a bullet list of on-chain circumstantial evidence (first coin bonded on
pump.fun, a wallet funded from Coinbase, a 24%-of-supply transfer to a wallet
attributed to Andrew Tate) plus the wordplay that would define the whole
saga — *"'BOB' in Contract Address: The contract address begins with 'BOB,' a
nod to the Terminator's alias, 'Uncle Bob.'"*
([26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md))

## The "BoB" Easter egg

The CA's first three characters, `BoB`, became the community's central piece
of self-generated proof almost immediately — tied to "Uncle Bob," the name
Arnold Schwarzenegger's character uses for himself in *Terminator 2*, and
stacked against the "EXTERMINATE" sound effect the community said played on
Tate Terminal notifications:

> "Uncle bob from terminator. What does the first 3 letters of our CA
> spell? / 'Bob'"
> — P, [27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md)

This reasoning gets repeated, chanted, and screenshotted for the rest of the
week (*"Our CA starts with bob"*, *"The BoB in CA was the thing that made it
tick for me"*, *"ca is BOB"*) and is still being invoked as shorthand
conviction over a year later — *"We are the prophets of the tateverse
ecosystem we knew from day one BOB"* (Gabriel, 20 Mar 2025). It is also,
notably, the *only* piece of "proof" anyone in the chat ever really had —
nobody in this material produces an actual tweet, DM, or public statement
from Andrew Tate confirming the address.

## "CA is your compass"

A second, shorter-lived chant emerges the evening of
[29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md), apparently triggered by
a Tate Terminal tweet:

> "THE TERMINAL LITERALLY TWEETED ca is your compass"

...which several members immediately turn into a call-and-response, Rafe
posting *"CA IS THE COMPASS THE DESTINATION IS THE MOON!!!!!"* four times in
a row within the same minute. Read plainly, the "compass" line was never
itself a confirmation — it's the terminal being cryptic again — but the
chat treated it as one more data point pointing at the `BoB` CA already in
circulation.

## The CA that never officially dropped

The dominant mood of the whole founding week, visible across hundreds of
near-identical messages, is people waiting for an announcement that never
cleanly arrives: *"When do yous reckon the ca will drop?"*, *"No ca no
sleep"*, *"Still no ca and no ticker"*, *"As long as CA is not posted the
show is still going on"*. The hoped-for reveal moment — the launch of Tate's
"AI campus" on 1 Nov — passed with nothing (*"It was just a theory that he
will post the ca at start of the campus"*, Dom,
[2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)).

Then, on [4–5 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md), Tate
Terminal's X bio did get a contract address added to it — but it was
`$TATE`'s (the rival "cabal" coin's), not TateAI's:

> "TATE TERMINAL GOT THE CA IN THE BIO / WHY NOBODY SELLING 😔 / EVEN TATE
> WANTS TO BUY MORE TATEAI 😂😂😂"
> — Deleted Account, [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)

The community's rationalization for not treating this as a loss is worth
recording verbatim, because it's the moment "confirmation" quietly stopped
being something anyone expected to receive:

> "well didnt he say terminal would 'post the ca' simply adding it to the
> bio isn't posting the ca is it ;) just my own personal reasoning"
> — Stride, [5 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md)

No further official CA event is recorded in this material. The `BoB` address
was never posted by `@tate_terminalbot`, by Andrew Tate, or by any verified
account — it became "the" CA entirely by community consensus and repetition,
not by any documented external confirmation.

## Copycat CAs and "is this the real one"

This chat was, unsurprisingly, targeted by (or at least confused with) other
addresses more than once:

- [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md) — Conall posts a
  screenshot: *"Join that telegram and there's a different CA being told."*
- [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md) — Anton describes
  another community figure, Issa, having *"accidently"* posted the *$TATE*
  CA into his own private group before deleting it. The next day, Pam 🐰
  characterizes the fallout: *"Issa is a retard he posted the Tate ca in his
  group right at the beginning and they sniped the whole supply and sell
  constantly"* ([31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)) — a
  source of ongoing "which CA is real" arguments between the `$TateAI` and
  `$TATE` camps for days afterward.
- [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md) — Limalemon, mid-argument:
  *"You cant trick us with fake ca niggers hahha"* — an explicit
  fake-CA callout, not just confusion between two real rival coins.
- **17 May 2026** — Widelton, recalling the launch period from over a year
  later: *"at first the x accoutns showed the real ca of nig termial, daddy
  terminal, tate terminal / after a while the rumbled it and changed it to
  whats now"* — a claim (unverified beyond this one message) that the rival
  terminal accounts' bios were altered after the fact.

The doubt never fully goes away. As late as
**28 Apr 2026**, someone is still asking cold, with no other context: *"Is
this the real CA?"* And the community's on-chain-forensics habit outlives
the launch hype by years — on
[15 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-15.md), Widelton posts a
detailed, professional-grade "attribution dossier" methodology (deployer
tracing, bytecode comparison, multisig checks) for verifying the CA's
provenance from scratch, assigning roles to different members ("A
(Deployer)... B (Liquidity)... C (Holders)... D (Infra)"). Sergei runs his
own version of this repeatedly through 2025 — cross-checking Solscan transfer
histories into the CA
([11 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-11.md)), questioning why a
Binance hot wallet sent SOL directly to it, and at one point investigating a
different, similarly-formatted address:

> "I played with this CA - qRUZaCpgxaRH1s5V6opjPA6Hnpv5BM37LqkDBw7pump / And
> found some familiar detail"
> — Sergei, [29 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-29.md)

He also flagged that the community's favorite piece of secondary "proof" —
that Jupiter's token verification tool linked `$TateAI` to the Tate Terminal
X account — didn't actually hold up under questioning, since Tate Terminal
never posted the CA anywhere Jupiter could have used to verify it: *"why
Jupiter support said that terminal X account verified TateAI coin? It needs
to post CA in bio or in post, but terminal never posted TateAI CA. How it
could be done?"* ([15 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-15.md))

## The CA today

By May 2026 the CA has become fully automated community infrastructure
rather than a live question. The bot's own self-description, posted
[11 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-11.md), lists posting the CA on
every new-member join as a named function: *"02 / Contract Address / On
each arrival I dispatch /ca and the canonical sticker. / Community
tradition."* From 13 May 2026 onward the raw logs show the bot (sender
`$TateAI Terminal`) posting `CA - BoBj68cWnCvzMNUKzJyR7Jq7tLM3v76D1pYL1E8rpump`
on a regular, near-daily cadence, and a `Safeguard` account has been
independently re-posting the bare address in reply to other messages on a
similar schedule since October 2025 — the address is now a fixed, constantly
re-affirmed fact of the chat rather than something anyone is actively
investigating.
