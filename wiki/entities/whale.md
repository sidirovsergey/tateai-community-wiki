# Whale 🐳

A chaotic, joke-heavy launch-era teenager whose entire meaningful presence —
roughly 95% of all 1,420 messages — lands in a sixteen-day window, [2
Nov](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md) to [18 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-18.md), with the single densest burst
on [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md) (over 250 messages in
one day). After that the account goes almost silent, with a handful of
scattered one-liners through early 2025 and a single nostalgic drive-by
message fourteen months later.

## An unreliable narrator by design

Whale's self-description is a moving target and clearly not meant to be
taken at face value: "Im 13 are u a pedo??????" ([5 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md)) sits alongside "23" and "2001"
minutes later, and "I bench 140kg / At 17" ([4 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)). The financial claims follow
the same pattern — alternating between "6figs kid here" flexing and
"Im down 27k" / "Down 29k" panic, sometimes within the same conversation.
Whale also repeatedly claims insider access ("I got 4 people watching
andrews x constantly so if he puts the ca on his x 250k buy instant" — [4
Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md), ❤×2 👍×1) and, more than
once, claims to personally control the trading bots selling into the chart
("Im the one that controls the bots on tateai" — [12 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md)) — bravado, not credible
testimony.

Dutch and German both surface mid-conversation ("Ich kann deutsch spreche,"
"Naar de maan," "Niemand gaat meer dumpen"), suggesting a Netherlands-based
poster code-switching for the bit rather than a language barrier.

## The bit that reads as sincere

Underneath the trolling, one thread reads as genuine conviction rather than
performance — Whale describes real, tracked losses and keeps buying anyway:

> "Bought total of 30k worth of tateai" ... "My wallet is sitting on 9,5k" ...
> "But im 100% sure this is the ticker" ... "If this wasn't the ticker i
> wouldn't put 30k init"
> — [11 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-11.md), 🔥×4 / 👏×2 / 👍×4 ❤×2

## Launch-week joke persona

A recurring bit is playing a wide-eyed newcomer from the rival `$TATE`
camp:

> "Hello , i come from $tate. Is this the ticker?"
> — [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md), 🤣×7 👍×1

And a paranoid-theorizing streak about Telegram accounts secretly being
Andrew Tate's bots, complete with "evidence" (accounts showing "last seen
recently" rather than bot-typical offline status) — [12 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md).

Like most of the launch-era chat, casual racial slurs and one-off bigoted
asides (anti-immigrant, anti-LGBTQ, election-related) are constant
background texture rather than an aberration — documented here plainly
rather than smoothed over, consistent with how this wiki treats [[t]] and
[[igelo]]'s language.

## "Lima" here means Limalemon, not Orchestra

Whale repeatedly addresses **`@LimaLemon777`** directly by tag and by name
— "Lima why u talking with the bot u fatty" ([5 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md)), "Lima wanna buy matching
lambos?" ([5 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md)) — and later
describes having been a top holder alongside them: "Me and lima where top
holders / But we splitted the accounts" ([27 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-27.md)). Per the disambiguation on
[[orchestra_lima_maxi]], this is [[limalemon]] (`@LimaLemon777`), a
different person from the "Orchestra | Lima MAXI" raid captain despite the
shared "Lima" name.

## Fade-out and a late return

Regular posting stops after [18 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-18.md); a rueful aside four weeks later
— "Fuck tate / Nigga lost me 30k / Not gonna buy any stupid coins hes gonna
promote" ([20 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-20.md)) — is close to
the last coherent thought in the record. After a few scattered one-liners
into February 2025, the account goes fully quiet until a single
out-of-nowhere reappearance over a year later:

> "Ive been gone for 9months anything happend? / And yall still active???
> Noway" ... "Good old days"
> — [9 Mar 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-03/2026-03-09.md), 😁×1 / ❤×6
