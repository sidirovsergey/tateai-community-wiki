# Ri -ShadowAssassin

The most extreme case of launch-window concentration on this wiki: all
1,557 messages fall inside a single thirteen-day span, [28
Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md) to [10 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-10.md) — averaging well over a hundred
messages a day — and then nothing further appears in this material. Other
members address them as "Ricky" ("Not just Ricky" — [31 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)), evidently short for "Ri."
