# B

Another one-letter display name — like [[t]], simply who this person is in
the raw logs, and a different individual from both T and [[ben]] (see
`SCHEMA.md` and those pages' notes on not conflating short/similar names).
B's entire documented presence — all 1,388 messages — falls inside the
founding rush and its immediate aftermath: [29 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md) through [7 Feb
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-07.md), roughly three and a half
months, then nothing further in this material.

## Raid-command spammer and evidence theorist

Like most of the launch-era volume on this wiki, B's baseline mode is
firing off the same slash-command raid tag five to fifteen times in a row —
`/BIG_DICK_VS_ERECTILE_DYSFUNCTION`, `/TATEAI_IS_THE_TICKER`,
`/The_ones_who_dont_raid_are_GAY`, `/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`,
`/FINAL_SHAKEOUT`, `/Believe`, `/TATEAI_TO_100m` — interleaved with genuine
"which ticker is real" theorizing in the same register as [[sergei]], T, and
others working the same puzzle that period (see [[launch-and-origins]]):

> "imo i think its picked the ticker in the early beginning of the whole
> thing and it was prompted to cause chaos which is to make it look as it is
> TATE but hide clues to TATEAI"
> — [8 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-08.md), ❤×2 👍×2

B also does real wallet/liquidity-pool analysis rather than pure vibes:

> "Raydium is the liquidity pool for the tokens. / One 4% wallet is tate or
> issa (4.35%) bby / The other 4% wallet is a whale / Then there is 3%
> wallet which is lima I think / And the some other whales"
> — [7 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-07.md), 👍×4

## The `/ban` habit

B's most distinctive tic: a bare `/ban` reply, aimed at a specific message,
posted over 30 separate times between [24 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-24.md) and [24 Jan
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-24.md) — far more often than any
ordinary member would have cause to. The pattern (always a one-word reply,
never explained, never followed up on) reads as someone actually holding
moderation power and using it as a running punctuation mark to end
arguments, rather than a joke repeated for its own sake.

## Diamond-hands prose, same copy-paste genre as everyone else's

B is one of the several named senders [[diamond-hands]] documents posting
the chat's florid "diamond hands aren't about X, they're about Y" prose-poem
register — raid/copy-paste text passed around the community, not a personal
literary invention:

> "the pursuit of 100x is a journey into the self, a quest to conquer fear
> and doubt. diamond hands are not just about holding, but about
> transcending the temporal, fixing your gaze on the horizon of abundance.
> embrace the chaos of the dex screener, for it is the crucible in which
> financial alchemy is performed."
> — [14 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-14.md)

## The Nov 22 livestream aftermath

After Andrew Tate's Nov 22 livestream event (referenced elsewhere in this
wiki as a pump-and-dump moment — see [[orchestra_lima_maxi]]), B's reaction
is characteristic of the chat's "we told you so" register toward anyone who
sold:

> "Why is everyone salty? What did I miss? Didn't just that happen what we
> expected/wanted? / Last thing we want is to be a fucking pump and dump /
> Chill out and hold your bags you retards"
> — [22 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-22.md)

Like the rest of the launch-era chat, casual slurs (racial, homophobic) are
constant background texture in B's posting, not an aberration — consistent
with how this wiki documents [[t]] and [[igelo]]'s language rather than
smoothing it over.

## Fade-out

Activity thins through January 2025 alongside the community-wide falloff
(see [[launch-and-origins]]), and stops entirely after a short, ordinary
exchange:

> "What's that?"
> — [7 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-07.md)

No further messages from B appear in this material.
