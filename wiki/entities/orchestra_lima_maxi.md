# Orchestra | Lima MAXI

A top-5-by-volume poster whose message history has an unusually sharp shape:
roughly 94% of all 4,671 messages fall in a single ten-week window, [4 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md) to [13 Jan
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-13.md), during which this poster acted
as the chat's self-appointed **raid captain**. After that window, activity
falls off a cliff — from dozens of messages a day to sporadic single-word
appearances spread across the following eighteen months.

## Phase 1 — raid captain (Nov 2024 – Jan 2025)

The dominant activity in this phase is organizing Twitter/X "raid" campaigns:
firing off the same slash-command tag ten to fifteen times in under a minute,
over and over, across the whole window — `/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`,
`/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_NOW`, `/work_for_it`, `/can_you_hold_in_the_fire`,
`/jeet_now_cry_later`, `/raid_work_for_your_bags` — interleaved with sticker
spam and genuine on-chain commentary (MEV activity, liquidation patterns,
wallet-tracking). The persona is equal parts drill sergeant and analyst:

## Phase 2 — the "Lower" era (2025–2026)

From mid-January 2025 on, appearances thin out drastically and the register
flattens to a single recurring word: **"Lower"** — wanting the price down
further before buying more — posted standalone, often the only message on a
given day, dozens of times through mid-2025 into at least December 2025.
Between the "Lower" drive-bys, a few longer, more reflective messages surface:

operation's decline: it had a real organizational structure (shifts, admin)

that way at least once — "Lima wisdom" ([16 Jul
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-07/2025-07-16.md)). **But there is a separate

"@HFU5588 @LimaLemon777 we need to run raids" and "@LimaLemon777 buy the

year later, still referring to them in the third person: "Only two geezers
who get clout... And @LimaLemon777" ([4 Dec
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-12/2025-12-04.md)).

This matters because [[cloud_fortune]]'s messages talk extensively about a
top holder called "Lima," consistently via the `@LimaLemon777` handle ("Lima
is Tate's Insider," "Never get shaken out @LimaLemon777 💪," etc.). **That
material is about the other person, not this page's subject**, despite the
shared "Lima" name — the two should not be conflated. `@LimaLemon777` does
not yet have their own wiki page as of this writing; treat `[[limalemon]]`
as an anticipated stub, not a resolved link.
