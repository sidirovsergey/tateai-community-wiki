# Overview — what this chat is

**Terminal of Tate $TateAI** is a Telegram community (164,440 messages,
[26 Oct 2024](../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md) – [15 Jul
2026](../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-15.md)) built around `$TateAI`, a memecoin
whose founding premise was that Andrew Tate ("Daddy," "Tate") had hidden
clues pointing to it as his real, community-run token — distinct from his
own official coin. See [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] for the full,
genuinely confusing map of names in and around this project.

## The shape of it

This is overwhelmingly a **launch-hype chat**: roughly 70% of every message
ever posted here happened in the first three weeks (see [[timeline]] for the
month-by-month numbers). The founding activity was amateur on-chain
detective work — parsing wallet transactions to figure out which of several
candidate tokens was the "real" one before it was ever officially confirmed.
[[sergei]], later the project's owner, was one of the people doing that
analysis. After the initial rush burned out (Dec 2024), the chat settled
into a small, steady core that's kept it alive at a fraction of the volume
ever since, with one secondary bump in Jan 2025 tied to the broader "AI
agent" crypto narrative.

## Culture

The tone is standard crypto-Telegram degen humor — heavy profanity, dark
jokes, constant "wen moon" energy — layered with a few genuinely distinct
running bits that outlasted the hype cycle:

- **"Be cool 😎"** — [[rgk1]]'s zen-holder catchphrase, present from the
  earliest days, later evolving into denser AI-agent-narrative prose.
- **A bare `:)`** — see [[smiley-heartbeat]] — is the single most common
  message shape in the whole archive (1 in ~26 messages), present from day
  one. The community's own bot later adopted the same `:)` as its heartbeat
  message, not the other way around.
- **[[fluxcapacitor]]** — a rare, high-impact recurring "mystery oracle"
  character, "HELLO WORLD" openers, all-caps cryptic pronouncements,
  reliably the best-received bit in the chat whenever it shows up.
- **Diamond-hands rhetoric** — see [[diamond-hands]] — the community's
  central holding-through-volatility ethos, expressed constantly and often
  poetically, especially by [[rgk1]].

## A word of caution reading this chat

Two things trip up naive keyword search over this archive, both documented
in detail on their own pages:

1. **"Deleted Account" is the single biggest "sender" by volume and is not
   one person** — it's Telegram's placeholder for anyone who'd deleted their
   account by export time. See [[deleted-accounts]].
2. **Names that look like they mean something usually don't, or mean
   something different than they appear to.** "Tate AI" as a chat sender is
   an unrelated rival-project shill-bot, not this community. Forwarded posts
   from "tate terminal" can render as if a person said them (a raw-log
   parsing bug) when they're actually reposted content. See
   [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] for the full map — this is not
   optional reading if you're answering questions about "who said what."
