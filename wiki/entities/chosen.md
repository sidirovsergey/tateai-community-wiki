# Chosen

A launch-era regular whose whole run — [3 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) through [14 Feb
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-14.md), plus one lone message
[25 May 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-05/2025-05-25.md) after a three-month
silence — sits squarely in the "believer who tracks Andrew Tate's wallets
for a living" mold shared with [[sergei]], T, Marcin Krukowski, BoB, and
others (see the open thread noted in `wiki/index.md`). What distinguishes
Chosen from that pack isn't a unique angle so much as sheer persistence of
the same two habits — raid participation and obsessive wallet-watching —
sustained for over three months, plus a screen name that turns out to be a
small piece of foreshadowing.

## Self-description

Self-identifies as a student, based in France, of Moldovan/Romanian/Russian
background ("Im a 🇲🇩 cracker born in 🇷🇺 living in 🇫🇷 ... Shiiii even got the
🇷🇴 passport" — [24 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-24.md)) and
explicitly not a big bag holder: "Thx but i aint all that loaded y'all im a
student already put most into rnt and daddy 😂" —
[3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md).

## The name, explained

Chosen never comments on their own screen name directly, but it lands as a
punchline in the middle of a long rant about the community's underdog
status relative to the much larger `$Tate` chat:

> "You guys realise we're 10x smaller than those $Tate nigs (even tho
> they're 75% bots) / AND WERE STILL THE ONLY ONES RADING / ... WERE WHAT WE
> CALL A COMMUNITY AND NOT A FUCKING MILK COW SCAM / BECAUSE WE BELIEVE IN
> SOMETHING, THAT ALL EVIDENCE LEADS TO US AND THAT WERE CHOSEN"
> — [29 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-29.md), ❤×5

Whether the name predates or postdates that belief isn't recoverable from
the logs, but it's a tidy fit either way.

## The wallet-watcher habit

The most consistent thread through Chosen's whole run is tracking Andrew
Tate's personal Solana wallets — posting addresses, transaction links, and
running commentary on SOL accumulation, framed as evidence for an eventual
`$TateAI` pump:

> "Andres main wallet : / 4jRX4iW2F5wBnfYMyB7RjS2PU5MjXrST3fB9DoV4BjHa /
> HES SELLING ALL SMALL COINS FOR SOL"
> — [15 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-15.md)

> "💸 TRANSFER / 🔹 Andrew's 13k SOL / 🔹Andrew's 13k SOL transferred 10,000
> SOL to 24vy...miZi ... Whats he cookin... 👨‍🍳"
> — [7 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-07.md)

This runs alongside the same repeated theory-crafting seen throughout the
chat's founding week (see [[launch-and-origins]]): that Andrew Tate
personally, or his dev "issa," is testing the community's patience on
purpose, and that Cobratate's [12 Dec
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-12.md) repost is proof positive:

> "Just a confirmation hes aware of us and that we're the right ticker :)"
> — [12 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-12.md), ❤×3

The same link gets re-posted as "TATE_KNOWS" / "Just a matter of time" as
late as [5 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-05.md), over three
weeks after it first ran — the belief clearly outlasted the news.

## Raid voice and slurs

Alongside the analysis, Chosen is a heavy participant in the launch era's
slash-command raid chants, posted in the same rapid-fire bursts documented
on [[community-culture]] — `/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`,
`/TATEAI_TO_100m`, `/BuyTATEAIorStayBROKE` — and uses slurs as constantly
and unremarkably as most of that cohort. One raid burst is aimed
specifically at a rival account:

> "GUYS LETS FKN RAID TATES POSTS FROM NOW ON / ILL TRY COMMENTING ON ALL
> HIS POSTS/REPLIES"
> — [28 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-28.md)

## The last message

After a dense run through mid-February 2025, Chosen goes silent. The very
last line in the archive attributed to them, over three months later, is
just a question with no follow-up:

> "Any news since crypto crash?"
> — [25 May 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-05/2025-05-25.md)

Nobody in the sampled log replies to it, and Chosen doesn't post again.
