# ET

An on-chain sleuth and background moderator whose voice is steadier and
more analytical than most launch-era posters — closer in register to
[[sergei]] or [[t]] than to the chant-and-spam crowd. Active from
[28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md), Sydney/Australia-based
(explicit about timezone and tax questions), with family roots in Bosnia he
discusses when weighing a move abroad. Presence tapers sharply after early
2025 into occasional messages, then a single isolated line over a year
later.

## Opening line as personal refrain

ET's very first message in the archive sets the tone for his whole run:

> "You don't lose until you sell 😊"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)

He returns to the same line, near-verbatim, weeks later as a rallying cry
to the room: *"You dont lose until you sell boys :)"*
([12 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md)) — see [[diamond-hands]]
for how widely shared this exact framing was across the chat.

## The BBY wallet investigation

ET is one of several members (alongside [[sergei]], [[t]], and others — see
the on-chain-forensics thread noted across this wiki) who spent months
tracking a specific wallet, "BBY," that they believed was linked to Andrew
Tate and responsible for dumping both `$TateAI` and the separate `$G` token.
A long, sustained investigative thread on
[26 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md) is the clearest record
of this work:

> "So again, we need confirmation on bby. Seeing as Tate is loud on his
> public wallet, happy to post it around, then we need a way for him to
> explain himself without burning him."
> — [26 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md)

> "He's dodging the bby wallet, so we need a precision strike, thats why I
> suggested a tip off to a creator or something."
> — [26 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md)

He keeps returning to this specific claim — that the BBY wallet received
500 million tokens and "nuked" the chart — well into January 2025, treating
it as unfinished business rather than a settled conclusion.

## Background moderator

ET describes doing quiet, unglamorous moderation work rather than seeking
credit for it:

> "I cant promote people to admins but I can ban them, tbh I am one that
> usually does it in the background, bans and mute."
> — [1 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-01.md)

> "I mute, ban and remove the scam messages when they are here."
> — [12 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-12.md)

## Conviction, stated plainly

Unlike the chat's more florid diamond-hands prose-poetry (see
[[diamond-hands]]), ET's version of the same sentiment is blunt and
personal, offered as an apology after a wallet-tracing post got
misread as bearish:

> "Sorry boys maybe my point got brought on the wrong way. I'm still
> holding my bags. I'm never gonna sell my fucking bags. I never fucking
> leaving. I don't wanna put down. I just thought it was like some good
> information and positive for us."
> — [7 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-07.md) (❤×4, 👌×1)

## Fade-out

Activity drops off after the Jan 2025 "AI agent" wave (see [[timeline]]),
down to a handful of messages through Feb 2025, then near-total silence —
one bare reaction gif in Feb 2025, and a single `😆` over a year later on
[15 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-15.md). His last substantial
message in this material reads as a genuine sign-off rather than routine
chat noise:

> "Hope all the lads are doing well. Numbers on a screen or not; what is it
> to be a man? / Now be one. / 🤝"
> — [26 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-26.md)
