# Marcin Krukowski

The most extreme case of launch-rush concentration among this chat's top
posters. Nearly all 2,362 of Marcin Krukowski's messages land inside a single
month — [26 Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md) to [25 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-25.md) — after which he posts exactly
twice more, on [6 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-06.md), and then
never appears again in the archive. He is present from the chat's second day
and is one of the loudest voices of the founding "which ticker is real"
puzzle-hunt (see [[launch-and-origins]]), but unlike [[rgk1]] or [[sergei]]
he does not survive into the chat's later, quieter eras.

## Self-identified, unlike most of the chat

Marcin is unusual for this corpus in repeatedly linking to his own named
Twitter/X account (`x.com/mkrukowski`) and asking the chat to raid his own
posts — a real, self-disclosed identity rather than an anonymous handle:

> "https://x.com/mkrukowski/status/1850280127113957436..."
> — [27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md), one of many such
> links he posts and asks the group to engage with.

He also names his background directly, early on:

> "I got my own business and sports career. Didn't look there much, i did
> tasks and things for fun :)"
> — [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)

## The wallet-tracker and the moderator

Like [[sergei]], Marcin spent the founding weeks doing amateur on-chain
sleuthing — tracking which wallets held `$TateAI`, watching transfers, and
treating the pattern as evidence:

> "Afteral only one thing u cant argue. $tateai is only one token moved from
> main adress and sent to new one. FOR WHAT? Just for fun? 1 from
> thousands?!"
> — [27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) (❤×2)

He also held moderator tools during this period (`/unban`, `/unmute`
commands appear repeatedly through the launch month, e.g. [2
Nov](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md) and [7
Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-07.md)).

## The manifesto

Marcin's single most distinctive habit is copy-pasting the same
diamond-hands "manifesto" verbatim across multiple days whenever conviction
in the chat wavered — this exact text appears at least three times:

> "Not selling. / Never. / Tate would not sell $tateai without purpose. / It
> would ruin his image if he just send $tateai to other wallet to rug as for
> pennies! / REMEMBER. / Believe."
> — first posted [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md) (💯×5),
> reposted the same day (🔥×5, ❤×2, 👍×2) and again [2 Nov
> 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md).

Consistent with that conviction, he made a standing pledge that never gets
followed up on in the archive (he's gone before there's any occasion to
redeem it):

> "If we hit 500mil im gonna tattoo Andrew Tate on my back"
> — [27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md) (❤×1)
