# Rival Tate coins

[[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] covers the core disambiguation — Andrew
Tate's own `$Tate`/`$DADDY` and the `$RNT` shill-bot coin — briefly. This
page goes wider: `$TateAI` was never the only Tate-branded token this chat
tracked, and members have spent 2.75 years alternately trash-talking,
allying with, and confusing themselves about the others.

## The named coins

- **`$Tate` / `$DADDY`** — Andrew Tate's own token, universally nicknamed
  **"the cabal coin"** or just **"cabal"** in this chat from day one
  (*"We should rise up and wage war against that Cabal Coin"* — Hustlin
  Dude, [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)). The chat's
  default posture toward it is hostile-competitive: constant real-time
  score-keeping of its price against `$TateAI`'s, mockery whenever it dips
  (*"$TATE = MC BROKIES"*, [29 Oct
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)), and, once `$TateAI`'s own
  price recovers, casual reabsorption (*"$Tate down, $Daddy up, just we are
  so fucking stable"* — Sergei, [6 Nov
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-06.md)) — i.e. `$Tate` and `$Daddy`
  get used near-interchangeably for Tate's own coin even though they are
  the same asset referenced two ways.
- **`$RNT` ("Real Nigger Tate")** — covered in
  [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] as the coin behind the "Tate AI" shill
  sender. Beyond that bot, `$RNT` is treated in this chat as one of three
  legitimate "OG Tate coin" communities, not a joke (see below).
- **`$TOPG`** — the most durable rival/sibling coin in this material,
  referenced from the founding week (*"Do you guys going to buy some
  $TopG?"* — Asiman, [26 Oct
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)) through the most recent
  logs in this archive ([29 Jun
  2026](../../raw/logs/2026-06/2026-06-29.md)). Unlike `$Tate`, `$TOPG` is
  treated with solidarity rather than rivalry — see below.
- **`$TRATE`** — mentioned exactly once in this material, inside a
  Sergei theory-post about a hypothetical sequence of "decoy" coins the
  terminal might roll through before landing on `$TateAI`
  ([8 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-08.md)). Too thin to say
  more than that it was floated as a name, not established as a real
  community.
- **`$G`** — a coin tied to a wallet ("4JR") that predates `$TateAI`'s own
  launch controversies; it gets an MEXC-listing mention around
  [26 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-26.md) and is occasionally
  listed alongside the other tickers (*"$tateai x $daddy x $G x $RNT x
  Tate"* — Deleted Account, [25 Nov
  2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-25.md)) but never gets sustained
  discussion of its own in this material.
- **`$BRUV`** — appears once, in a Gabriel list of Tate-adjacent tickers
  (*"$Daddy $TRW $RNT $BRUV $G …"* — [29 Apr
  2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-29.md)). No other mentions found;
  flagged here rather than given its own section because the material
  genuinely doesn't support more.
- **`$TRW`** — the rumored Real World platform token, covered in full in
  [[the-real-world-trw]] rather than duplicated here.

## "The cabal" vs. the brotherhood: two different rivalries

The chat draws a real distinction between `$Tate`/`$DADDY` — mocked,
fought, occasionally "flipped" in market cap as a point of pride — and
`$RNT`/`$TOPG`, which get folded into `$TateAI`'s own mythology rather than
opposed. Sam's clearest statement of this, posted the same week as the
"cabal coin" hostility was at its peak, reframes all three non-`$Tate`
coins as a single family:

> "Tate was only ever in three OG Tate coin TGs, $RNT, $DADDY and $TOPG /
> $RNT - REVOLUTION / $DADDY - REVELATION / $TOPG - RESURRECTION / The
> brotherhood of one man"
> — Sam, [13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md)

[[widelton]] produces a more developed version of the same idea nearly a
year later, explicitly modeling the ecosystem as tiered rather than
competitive:

> "🌐 The Tate Religion / The Coins: Daddy – symbolizes the Alpha Father,
> ultimate authority. RNT (Real Nigger Tate) – creates an exclusive
> brotherhood, radical identity. TATEAI – the higher brain, the eternal
> algorithm that outlives Tate the man. / The Structure: Daddy = The
> Father. RNT = The Brotherhood/Community. TATEAI = The Algorithm/The God."
> — Widelton, [29 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-29.md)

[[sergei]] states the same hierarchy more tersely, and notably includes
`$G`, `$TRATE`, and anything else outside the core three under one
dismissive label:

> "Real daddy_term coin is $Daddy, nig_term is $RNT, others are scam"
> — Sergei, [23 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-23.md)

## `$TOPG` specifically: the closest thing to a sister community

`$TOPG` gets treated as kin rather than competition for the whole span of
this archive. Sam becomes the chat's resident `$TOPG` liaison from early
2025 onward, holding both tokens and explicitly bridging the two
communities (*"I have such deep conviction in both $TATEAI and $TOPG"* —
[10 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-10.md); *"particularly today,
and then Tate drops the image again... We've been bringing our communities
together lately"* — [13 Jan
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-13.md)). [[sbzgh]] continues that
role into 2026, per their own entity page, and the sentiment shows up as
an explicit chat-wide statement, not just Sam's personal view:

> "tate ai bros = top g bros / same family, our insight has led each of us
> to one of the two coins, or even both coins for some. i hope that after
> the pump, we will all stay in touch and help each other make even more
> money"
> — sbzgh, [28 Sep 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-09/2025-09-28.md)

## Getting banned for cross-promotion

Loyalty to `$TateAI` while participating in rival groups had real
consequences, documented in detail on two entity pages rather than
repeated here: [[crpt4l]] ran a second account inside the `$TATE` group
specifically to argue for `$TateAI` and was banned for it
([3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md)); [[t]] describes
still being banned from the same group seven months later. They were not
isolated cases — this material also shows Asiman (*"I just got banned from
$Tate group, i proud"* — [30 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)), Sam Garsfontein CPF
([6 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-06.md)), and AJ ⚡️, banned
*"Asked terminal why it sent coins to wallet that's top Tateai holder"*
([17 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-17.md)) — a recurring,
almost routine pattern in the founding weeks rather than three unrelated
incidents.
