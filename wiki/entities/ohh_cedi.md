# Ohh Cedi

A launch-week regular who, unusually, never really leaves. First message
[29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md) — stacked bare `:)`s
and GIF spam typical of that first week (see [[smiley-heartbeat]]) — and the
sampled material runs continuously through
[8 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-08.md), nearly a full year
later, without the long silent gaps that end most launch-era voices'
stories on this wiki.

## Voice: the patience-preacher

Ohh Cedi's defining mode from the first week onward is hyping the room
through every dip with a specific, repeated argument: the price is low
*because* it's a shakeout, and holding through it is what separates "OG's"
from "jeets." Typos and run-on sentences are constant and part of the
voice, not incidental:

> "When this all goes tits up rocket high we need to keep this group as
> OG's and look for different options in the market we have shown our
> diamond balls at the moment"
> — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)

> "if this ever pumps and were at a high mcap and we see all these new guys
> being bullish wanting to hit a billion of whatever and the chat goed
> crazy we'll look back here when were with a small group of 50 talkers
> holding it down"
> — [14 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-14.md) (❤×5)

Like several other launch-week figures on this wiki, Ohh Cedi's in-group
address routinely includes slurs used as casual filler alongside "boys" and
"G" — this is a load-bearing, uncomfortable feature of the voice worth
flagging plainly (per this wiki's convention on [[igelo]] and
[[widelton]]) rather than smoothing over, not an isolated incident.

## A specific, dated moment: watching Tate's stream live

On [22 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-22.md), during an Andrew
Tate livestream the chat watched in real time hoping for a coin mention,
Ohh Cedi's running commentary turns sharply critical of Tate himself rather
than the usual patience-preaching:

> "But tate doesnt know shit about crypto 😂 what is slippage the guy said
> hahaha"
> — 22 Nov 2024 (🤣×1)

> "That was an L stream he acted big shot and every coin he wanted to pump
> nothing did 😂"
> — 22 Nov 2024

This is a genuine break from the usual cheerleading register — for one
night, Ohh Cedi is openly unimpressed with the person the whole chat is
theoretically organized around.

## The long tail: a daily greeting ritual

From early 2025 onward, Ohh Cedi settles into something close to a daily
fixture — a "Gm fuckers" / "Sup fuckers" greeting posted most days,
frequently drawing real reaction counts, running essentially unbroken
through the sampled material into October 2025:

> "GOOOODMORNING FUCKERSSSSS"
> — [4 Feb 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-02/2025-02-04.md) (🤝×2)

> "Gm fuckers always good to see some people come in here an stir the pot
> hahaha fuck off"
> — [15 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-15.md)

This puts Ohh Cedi in the same small category as [[cloud_fortune]] (daily
"GM :)") and [[widelton]] (near-daily "Gm" plus `/ca` ritual) — chat members
whose main long-run function becomes showing up reliably rather than
saying anything new. Unlike those two, Ohh Cedi never becomes a moderator
or builder; the page is honest about that — the throughline here is
duration and consistency, not escalation into a bigger community role.
