# Cloud Fortune

One of the highest-volume posters in the whole archive and, by tenure, one of
the longest-running: first message [28 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md) — the second day of the chat's
pre-launch ticker-hunt saga — and still actively posting as of [12 Jul
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-12.md), the last date in this material.
Where [[rgk1]] is the chat's zen-holder poet, Cloud Fortune is its
uninterrupted true believer: the throughline across nearly two years is
absolute, performative conviction that Andrew Tate is about to make a move,
expressed almost every single day regardless of price action.

## Voice — "Bruv," ":)", and endless anticipation

The signature tics are consistent from the first week onward and barely
change over 21 months:

- **"Bruv" / "Bruvs"** as address, constantly.
- **A trailing `:)`** on a huge share of messages — see
  [[smiley-heartbeat]] — functioning as Cloud Fortune's personal sign-off as
  much as the chat's general one.
- **"GM :)" / "G Fucking M :)"** as a near-daily ritual greeting, especially
  from mid-2025 onward.
- **Hostility toward sellers ("JEETS")** paired with contempt for anyone who
  loses conviction on a red day.
- **A permanent "something is coming" posture** — reading delays, silences,
  and ordinary price moves as deliberate signals from Tate — that never
  resolves and never seems to erode the poster's confidence.

> "Don't get shaken out, this is perfectly planned by Tate to get rid of the
> JEETS and give everyone a last entry 🔥👀"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)

> "Always funny to see some people lose conviction wen a coin tanks haha,
> they need to be comfortable in red... it's not like we got the wrong
> Ticker 😉"
> — [8 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-08.md), ❤×5

> "It's over... let's all sell / 💀 / I'm done 💀"
> — [25 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-25.md)

The "it's over 💀" bit recurs constantly as an ironic, self-aware doom gag —
almost always followed within days by a fresh "it's coming" post, never as a
genuine capitulation.

> "Feels good holding TateAI now :)"
> — [13 Jun 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-06/2025-06-13.md), ❤×5

> "So the deadline is likely August 👀, / I truly have no idea what's coming,
> / Hold on bruvs, / We are almost there :)"
> — [5 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-05.md), ⚡×6

That last quote is representative of the long tail: eighteen months after
launch, the register hasn't moved an inch — still reading tea leaves, still
certain the payoff is imminent, still signing off with `:)`.

## The TRW pivot

From roughly mid-2025 onward, a growing share of Cloud Fortune's posts stop
being original chat commentary and become straight reposts of The Real World
(Andrew Tate's `jointherealworld.com` education platform) marketing copy —
"EMERGENCY MEETING" announcements, Tate quote-graphics, and enrollment links,
often with no added commentary beyond a `:)` or "GM." Examples: [16 Sep
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-09/2025-09-16.md) ("Bandwidth is limited... THIS IS
YOUR WARNING. Join now: www.jointherealworld.com"), [20 May
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-20.md) (EM livestream links posted twice
in one day). This tracks a broader pattern in the chat's later years — see
[[community-culture]] — where TRW promotion and TateAI speculation become
increasingly interchangeable in the daily posting rhythm, at least for this
poster.
