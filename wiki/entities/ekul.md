# Ekul🫡

One of the earliest arrivals in the archive — his first message lands
[27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md), the chat's second day
— and one of the more distinctive launch-era voices despite a short run: all
861 messages fall within a single month, ending
[25 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-25.md), after which he never
appears again in the sampled material. British slang throughout ("mate,"
"G," "boys," "🫡" as a running salute that doubles as his display-name
emoji), constant self-interruption for work ("Just in work… so can't stare
at the Tg 😂"), and a handful of recurring bits carried the whole month.

## The Pam bit

From his first week, Ekul runs a standing gag demanding "celebratory tits"
from two named women in the chat, `@pamelalu` ("Pam") above all:

> "Where's Pam 🐰 with those celebratory tits"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md), 🔥×2 ❤×1 🤣×1

> "Boys…. @pamelalu still here? / Most important question"
> — [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md), ❤×2

The bit runs for weeks as a genuine recurring roll-call, not a one-off.

## Rivalry raider against $Tate

Ekul is one of the chat's most active participants in the running
feud with the rival `$Tate` ("cabal") group documented in
[[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] and [[launch-and-origins]] — needling
their chart, mocking their holders as "fags" and "cucks," and getting
himself banned from their Telegram for it, which he wears as a badge:

> "I got banned for call them fags"
> — [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md)

> "Wish I was banned from their Tg… but then calling them fags was good"
> — [1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md)

The Titanic/lifeboat framing also independently recurs in his material —
"We've always been the life boat in the tatetanic scenario"
([17 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-17.md)) — echoing the same
"Tatetanic" Easter-egg reading [[jahn]] develops at more length on his own
page; the two don't appear to be quoting each other, just drawing on the
same chat-wide meme.

## The exit fantasy

His clearest single passage is a daydream about quitting work entirely once
the coin pays off:

> "Keep the updates flying in here boys. I'm in work 😂🤦🏽‍♂️ hopefully for
> the last time" / "It's my own business… so I'll just lock the door and
> never look back" / "Jet ski off into the sun" / "It's been a wild ride
> boys 🫡" / "See you in Dubai boys"
> — [7 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-07.md), 💯×4 ❤×1

## The Jakob bit

On [13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md) Ekul is one of several
members who spin up a short-lived collective hallucination around a name —
"Jakob" — that spirals into a running joke pinning the identity on
[[limalemon]]: *"@LimaLemon777 is Jakob"* / *"Fuck… I'm Jakob"* / *"Your
names Andrew."* No lasting significance, but a good example of how quickly
this chat could generate and abandon an in-joke in a single sitting.

## Slurs and register

Like several other launch-era regulars on this wiki, Ekul's casual register
is dense with homophobic ("gay," "fag") and, less often, racial slurs used
as filler ("Niggers niggers niggers… what's new?" —
[13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md)) — noted plainly rather
than smoothed over, consistent with how this wiki treats the same pattern on
[[widelton]] and [[jahn]].
