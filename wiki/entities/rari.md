# Rari

The chat's most sustained *analytical* voice during the founding ticker-hunt
week — less caps-lock hype, more running theory, cross-referencing, and
French-language asides. Nearly all 890 messages land in a three-week window,
[30 Oct](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md)–
[18 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-18.md), after which Rari goes
silent for over a year and a half — then resurfaces for three messages on
[11 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-11.md), the single longest
reappearance gap found among the entity pages on this wiki so far.

## The ticker-hunt's on-chain-and-Easter-egg theorist

Rari runs both investigative tracks [[launch-and-origins]] describes —
wallet-tracing and symbolic reading — often in the same message, and is one
of the clearer voices articulating *why* the community settled on
`$TateAI` over the rival `$Tate`:

> "somebody should make a list of all the clues so far, it literally
> wouldn't make any sense for it not to be TATEAI at this point"
> — [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md), 🔥×1

> "I understand what Murad meant when he said that the cults are the ones
> sustaining the charts by buying the dips haha. Guess I'm part of the cult
> now"
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md), 👏×2 ❤×1

That second line is also cited on [[community-culture]] as an example of
the chat's explicit self-aware "we are a cult" framing — Rari is its
clearest single source.

## "Trade in the currency of chaos" — a real Rari message, not a forward

On [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) Rari posts a
screenshot captioned with a quotation:

> "trade in the currency of chaos"
> — [3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md), 👏×5

The raw log confirms this is Rari's own message (`Rari`, not a forwarded
`tate terminal …` sender string) — he's quoting a `tate_terminal` tweet
screenshotted into a photo, marked with quotation marks in his own caption.
This is worth stating explicitly because [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]]
flags exactly this kind of line as a place earlier ad-hoc testing produced a
wrong answer; here the quotation-mark convention and the raw sender field
agree, so there's no misattribution risk — Rari is a genuine chat
participant relaying content, not the forwarded-content source itself. A
`Deleted Account` reply moments later ("Raid this guys") treats it as raid
material, consistent with how the community used tate_terminal quotes.

## TRW advocate

Rari is a genuine, detailed promoter of Andrew Tate's "The Real World"
(TRW) throughout his run — not just a slogan-poster but someone offering
specifics and fielding questions from newcomers:

> "Bro, it's really good if you put in the work. Some would say the lessons
> by themselves are basic, but they're straight to the point and there's a
> whole community pushing you to be better and work harder. / 100% worth
> it. Some students are making bank. / And increasing your Power Level will
> get you a free airdrop when Tate launches TRW token which will pay
> dividends."
> — [31 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-31.md)

He also switches fluently into French mid-thread with at least one other
French-speaking member ("Je touche pas $TATE avec un baton moi lol, trop de
signaux qui pointent vers TateAI imo" —
[1 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-01.md)).

## Willing to entertain the "Tate is scamming" hypothesis

Unusually for this chat's overwhelmingly pro-Tate register, Rari engages
seriously with a hypothetical that Tate could be the bad actor, in a
back-and-forth about the ethics of what happened to `$Tate` holders:

> "Yes but that is akin to scamming. He redirected people towards Tate
> terminal which retweeted a token, then posted the CA and TG, Tate joined
> the TG and said his AIs would push the coin.. that is scamming to me if
> it just sank."
> — [8 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-08.md)

He also relays a piece of content worth flagging precisely rather than
loosely: the raw log for
[5 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md) shows Rari prefacing it
with "Remember!" and then the line itself tagged
`` `Rari` (forwarded from `Tate`) `` — a genuine Telegram forward, not
Rari's own words: *"I have a button I can press to double daddy right now.
But its about timing, I press it at the right time and we 20x. […] Its
about TIMING"* (❤×2). He reposts the same content again, this time typed
out rather than forwarded, on
[7 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-07.md) — using it both times as
evidence for a personal theory about when Tate would reveal `$TateAI`'s
ticker.

## A near-two-year gap, then a brief return

After [18 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-18.md), Rari disappears
from the sampled material entirely. The next and last messages found are
dated [11 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-11.md) — three short
messages about TRW campus content and recent `$DADDY`/`$RNT` trades, with
no mention of `$TateAI` itself:

> "Yeah and the brothers said they had something big planned at the end of
> year + rumble wallet needed, etc. Sounds like tour might be happening
> soon / Btw I made some money trading daddy and RNT last week because you
> shared bullish news in here so thanks for this too lol, timing was
> perfect"
> — [11 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-11.md), ❤×3

Read plainly: Rari stayed inside the broader Tate/TRW crypto orbit long
after leaving this specific chat's active conversation, and dropped back in
only briefly, on unrelated business.
