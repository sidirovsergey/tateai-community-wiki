# E

Another single-letter sender — a different, distinct individual from
[[t]], [[ben]], and [[b]] (see `SCHEMA.md` on not merging short names). E's
first message, [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md), makes
this one of the earliest-dated voices in the whole archive, present from
the "which ticker is real" scavenger-hunt days documented in
[[launch-and-origins]]. The material runs continuously, if thinning
sharply, all the way to [31 Oct
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-31.md) — a full year of presence — and
splits cleanly into two very different-sounding phases.

## Phase 1 (Oct–Dec 2024): fluent, analytical, moderator-adjacent

Early E reads as a native or highly fluent English speaker running
active theorizing and, apparently, real moderation power:

> "Admins be vigilant" / "Ban any spam"
> — [27 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-27.md)

> "I banned him" / "Admins stay vigilant"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md)

> "Don't care about any "power". Top 25 holder and been here since 200k"
> — [26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md), 🔥×1

> "Don't understand panic sellers, if you are a panic seller you need to
> self analyze. It's a form of self sabotage. Only invest with conviction"
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md), ❤×2 🔥×1

This phase is dense with the same "$TATE isn't pumping, therefore $TATEAI
is the real ticker" evidence-chasing every other launch-era poster on this
wiki was doing (see [[launch-and-origins]]), plus a moderator's habit of
policing tone: "If you start talking rude or insult anyone you will be
kicked immediately" ([26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)).

## Phase 2 (Jan 2025 onward): a different-sounding voice

Starting abruptly around [4 Jan
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-04.md), the register shifts hard:
sentences pick up mid-word noun capitalization typical of German-native
English ("We Need to buy more," "Best men," "Since when?," "What time I
bought?"), and outright German surfaces periodically — "Ich kann deutsch
spreche" territory carried further, e.g. "Ehrlich stark" / "Viel geredet
wenig gehalten" ("Honestly strong" / "Talked a lot, held little") on [7 Aug
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-07.md), and "Mein kampf?" /
"Einzige Buch was ich brauche" as a dark joke on [19 Mar
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-19.md). The moderator behavior from
Phase 1 doesn't reappear. Whether this reflects the same person's voice
loosening up over months, a different household member using the same
account, or something else isn't determinable from chat content alone —
flagged here rather than smoothed into one consistent narrator, the same
way [[fluxcapacitor]]'s register shift is flagged without asserting an
identity change.

A representative moment from this phase — anxious, sincere, and off-topic
from the ticker hunt entirely:

> "what do you guys think when will war come to europa?" / "need to build a
> villiage in the forest with a lot of weapons"
> — [11 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-11.md), 🔥×2

## Becoming a daily ":)" fixture

From mid-2025 on, E's posting rate drops to near-daily and the content
narrows almost entirely to the chat's [[smiley-heartbeat]] ritual — bare
`:)`, "Gm :)", occasional stickers — the same pattern [[cloud_fortune]]
sustains for years. Unlike most drive-by `:)` posters, E's late messages
consistently draw real engagement (🔥×9, 🔥×8, 🔥×7 on ordinary "Gm :)"
posts through August–September 2025), suggesting a recognized, welcomed
fixture rather than noise:

> "Love how active the group still is. REAL COMMUNITY"
> — [10 Jul 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-07/2025-07-10.md), ❤×3 🔥×2 ❤‍🔥×1
