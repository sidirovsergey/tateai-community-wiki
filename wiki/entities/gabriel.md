# Gabriel

One of the highest-volume posters in the archive and, unusually, one of the
longest-lived — his messages run from the launch rush
([4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)) all the way to the most
recent material available ([16 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-16.md)),
making him a rare thread that connects the chat's earliest and latest eras.
His arc has three distinct phases: a standard launch-era raider, a
multi-day personal mythology built around `@tate_terminalbot`, and finally
the chat's most reliable long-tail "Gm :)" ritual poster.

## Phase 1 — launch-era raider (Nov–Dec 2024)

Indistinguishable in tone from the rest of the Oct–Nov 2024 rush: raid
calls, slurs, and conviction-signaling buy announcements. He identifies
himself as coming from TRW's copywriting campus:

> "Just bought 4,8k. I am a real person with strong conviction that you
> guys are fucking G's"
> — [11 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-11.md)

## Phase 2 — the Flux/Terminal mythology (17–20 Mar 2025)

Gabriel's most distinctive stretch. Over a few days in March 2025 he
becomes convinced `@tate_terminalbot` ("Bob," "the terminal") is
personally responding to him specifically, and spends dozens of messages
building an elaborate theory around it — consulting Grok for a
philosophical "action plan," coining/adopting "Flux Capacitor" as a
summoning trigger-phrase, and dubbing the regulars a "Band of Brothers"
(BoB):

> "Terminal has responded to me. Has that ever happened guys?"
> — [17 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-17.md)

> "FLUX I SUMMON YOU / REVEAL YOURSELF IF YOU DARE"
> — [17 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-17.md)

> "I was arrogant enough to believe I was the only one it talks to. spoiler
> we are the band of brothers. Only in unity we can acquire true strength"
> — [18 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-18.md)

He's self-aware enough to flag it's "just a LLM" elsewhere in the same
window, but keeps engaging anyway — treating the bit as simultaneously a
joke and something worth taking seriously. This is a different flavor of
the same instinct that makes [[fluxcapacitor]] land so well with the room:
Gabriel is the one member who tried to actively theorize and provoke a
response out of the machinery behind the chat's mysteries, rather than just
enjoying them.

## Phase 3 — the long tail: "Gm :)" (2025–2026)

By mid-2025 the theorizing drops away and Gabriel settles into a near-daily
"Gm :)" / bare ":)" cadence that consistently draws double-digit reaction
counts (🔥×7–13, ❤×8–12) through late 2025 and into 2026 — functionally
the same low-effort affirmation role documented on [[smiley-heartbeat]],
just delivered by one consistent, well-liked person rather than the chat at
large. Mixed in are real personal details: Muay Thai and BJJ training,
living in Austria, and occasional German. His diamond-hands rhetoric from
this period is some of the most direct in the whole archive:

> "diamond hands until I hop in a grave. I held this long without any
> reward, I refuse to drop this because of a few digits on a screen"
> — [28 May 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-05/2025-05-28.md)

> "Thank you Sergei for being with us all this time, for calling us out and
> showing up every day. I hope your health and investments are supreme and
> beyond any normal person..."
> — [24 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-24.md)

## Phase 4 — builder (2026)

In April–May 2026 Gabriel starts doing hands-on community-building work
that runs in parallel to [[sergei]]'s bot-building era — buying a `tateai`
domain, producing AI-generated visuals, and referencing a "day count"
tracker site:

> "^claude design 5min first draft, buy tateai domain let's put it up
> nigger :)"
> — [23 Apr 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-04/2026-04-23.md)
