# Jahn

A pure launch-era voice: every one of Jahn's 805 messages falls between
[29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md) and
[13 Dec 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-12/2024-12-13.md), with the overwhelming
bulk concentrated in the first two frantic weeks. He never appears again
anywhere in the sampled raw material after that December date — no
farewell, no explanation, just silence. Unlike [[dom]] (a similarly
pure-launch-window poster), Jahn does have a few distinct, recurring angles
worth recording rather than just volume.

## Self-disclosure: a young Australian fighter

Between the hype spam, Jahn drops unusually concrete personal detail for
this era of the chat:

> "I do bjj and Muay Thai so you can say mma, my gym is very mma focused
> I've got a bjj comp on the 10th and a Muay Thai fight on the 30th but
> shiiit $TATEAI is real"
> — [30 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-30.md), 🔥×1

He also says he's "from Aus" and not eligible to vote in the concurrent
US election he's cheering on, and gives inconsistent, likely-joking answers
about his age within the same week ("I wouldn't know I'm only 12," then
days later "16" when someone else asks the room) — not resolvable from the
material, and probably not meant to be.

## The scam-bait bit

Jahn runs a short, self-aware sarcasm routine offering to "help" a newcomer:

> "Yeah bro just send me your private keys and I'll deposit 20 Solana / Just
> dm me / Very safe 100% secure"
> — [4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md)

...immediately clarified by other members as sarcasm, and followed minutes
later by Jahn volunteering he'd actually been scammed for $800 once and "now
I love to kill scammers." He's also one of the voices who explicitly plays
newcomer-greeter: "Hello and welcome to the group / I am here to help / Any
questions and I will attempt to answer them within my limited understanding"
([2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)).
