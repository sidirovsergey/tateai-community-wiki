# BoB

A real, individual chat member — self-described as 16 years old and living
in Syria when the chat launched ([29 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md): *"Im 16,"* and separately *"Im
super new to crypto im kinda scared and i also live in syria so its ultra
hard for me to buy crypto"*). One of the founding-era posters (first message
[26 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md)) and, unlike most of
that cohort, a genuine long-haul participant — still posting as late as [21
Jun 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-06/2026-06-21.md).

## Important: this is a name collision, not the ticker

**Read [[tate-tateai-terminal-namespace]] and `SCHEMA.md` before citing
anything about "BoB" without checking which one is meant.** The community
separately uses "BoB" as an informal nickname for the project's own contract
address, which starts `BoBj68cWnCvzMNUKzJyR7Jq7tLM3v76D1pYL1E8rpump` — see
[[contract-address]] — and for a "three things" visual-clue theme from the
founding puzzle hunt (three letters in "BoB," three segments in the logo,
three lines in a `:)`, discussed at length by [[jame_s]] on [5 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-05.md) and [6 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-06.md)). This chat member's display
name is coincidentally identical to that nickname, and he is visibly aware
of and amused by the coincidence rather than being its subject:

> "Bob huh? 😂" [reacting to a photo, at the exact moment the CA/logo
> "three things" theory was circulating]
> — [28 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-28.md) (🔥×5, 👀×3)

> "The bob thing is actually wild"
> — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)

> "Bob the niggger 🎶🎶"
> — [2 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-02.md)

By 2026 the ambiguity resurfaces and is genuinely unresolved in the sampled
material: on [24 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-24.md) he writes
*"We need different pictures for bob and the terminals profiles"* — which
could mean a mascot/avatar concept called "Bob" distinct from himself, or
could be a joke about his own profile picture. Treat any "BoB" reference in
this chat as needing this check, the same way [[deleted-accounts]] and
"Tate AI" the sender need their own disambiguation.

By 2026, BoB has settled into the same "amateur detective" tradition as
[[sergei]] and early [[jame_s]], digging through court filings and wallet
addresses looking for connections to the project's lore:

> "I think i found something interesting... Look up Case
> 1:23-cv-00533-RP. Its somehow connected to the wallet address that darko
> kareem sent... There is a document that was filed as evidence against the
> defendants in this case that had 3 pages of eth addresses containing the
> addres that darko sent"
> — [24 May 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-24.md)

He also appears to hold some elevated trust or informal permissions by this
point (asked about pinning messages, [15 May
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-05/2026-05-15.md): *"Nope sadly not only the
owner can, we both have the same permissions i think"*), though this isn't
confirmed as a formal admin role.
