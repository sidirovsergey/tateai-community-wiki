# "Deleted Account" is not a person

`Deleted Account` is the single highest-volume "sender" in the entire
archive — 33,803 messages, roughly 1 in 5 of everything ever posted. **It is
not one individual.** It's Telegram's placeholder label, applied identically
to every user who had deleted their Telegram account by the time this export
was taken. An unknown number of *different* real people are merged under
this one name.

## What this means practically

- Never write or imply a single personality, opinion, or position for
  "Deleted Account" — any two `Deleted Account` messages, even seconds
  apart, could be different people.
- Don't count `Deleted Account`'s message volume toward "most active
  participant" claims as if it were a person (it's why this wiki's
  entity pages skip straight past it to [[limalemon]] and below,
  despite it topping the raw count).
- A `Deleted Account` message can still be cited as **content** — "someone
  in the chat said X on this date" — just never as **someone's** opinion by
  name.
- The high volume itself is a real, citable fact about the chat's churn: a
  large share of this community's early, high-volume participants (the
  Oct–Nov 2024 launch rush, see [[launch-and-origins]]) are no longer
  reachable/identifiable as anything more than this label.
