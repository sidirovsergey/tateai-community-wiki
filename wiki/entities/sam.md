# Sam

The chat's resident philosopher-theologian and, unusually, a *dual*
community member — a long-serving figure in another "hidden Tate coin,"
`$TOPG` (also called BURN THE WORLD / Phoenix Rising), who joined
`$TateAI` in [mid-November 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md)
after conversations with [[limalemon]] and stayed active in both
communities for the rest of the archive. Sam's presence spans nearly the
entire dataset — [29 Oct 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-29.md)
through at least [5 Jul 2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-05.md), about
21 months — making him one of only a handful of members with a
near-continuous multi-year presence (compare [[widelton]], [[cloud_fortune]]).
Where most long-haul posters here settle into terse `:)`-only lurking (see
[[smiley-heartbeat]]), Sam is the opposite: his messages stay long,
elaborate, and evangelistic for the entire run.

## Arrival: the credentialed skeptic-turned-convert

Sam's real entrance is a long, deliberately reasonable-sounding post
explaining he'd ignored both `$TateAI` and its rivals until a specific
signal changed his mind:

> "Before I spoke with Lima, I wasn't even bothering to look into either AI
> coin, didn't appeal to me. But what I always remembered thinking was
> 'ticker's been mentioned, ca on X, listing and Tate in channel, but
> people are still cautious and unsure'... that spoke volumes to me, humans
> are good at sensing a vibe that's not quite right"
> — [12 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-12.md) (👍×5)

He's explicit from day one that he's not exclusively a `$TateAI` holder —
he holds `$TOPG` too, and frames the two as siblings rather than rivals,
a position that draws immediate suspicion from some members and that he
defends at length, repeatedly, for the next year and a half:

> "I am invested in $TATEAI, I am invested in one other Tate coin. I have
> been holding it for over 5 months... You may think I'm an idiot or
> shilling/trolling, but I assure you I'm not. I'm on a journey, and I
> respect G's when I see them."
> — [13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md) (👍×5)

## "The Brotherhood of One Man"

Sam's signature contribution is a sustained, self-consistent mythology
built across hundreds of messages: that Andrew Tate has deliberately seeded
multiple "hidden" coins across the Solana ecosystem, each a different
"incarnation," with `$TateAI` as his literal AI form:

> "There were only 3 OG Tate coins with Tate as admin... $RNT - REVOLUTION
> / $DADDY - REVELATION / $TOPG - RESURRECTION / The brotherhood of one man
> / Chaos and opportunity / BURN THE WORLD"
> — [13 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-13.md)

> "Tate wanted to become AI. He said in his first terminal EM... That's
> clearly $TATEAI, with all the Tristan, sell and Jupiter primary evidence
> / But he also has human incarnations... I think Tate man and Tate machine
> will conquer the world together"
> — [18 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-18.md)

The register — dense, quasi-biblical, heavy on words like "perspicacious"
and "indefatigable" — is his own sustained personal style rather than
copy-paste raid text (contrast the identical-text chants documented on
[[diamond-hands]] and [[community-culture]]), and it doesn't dilute over
time. A representative later line, in 2026:

> "The breadcrumbs were to find problem solvers / The clues were to find
> the perspicacious / The delays were to find the indefatigable / The
> hopelessness was to find the resolute / The noise was to find the wise /
> The red pill was to find the serious / The 16th Law was to find the
> winners"
> — [9 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-09.md) (❤×8)

## Cross-community bridge-builder

Sam is also independently notable as the chat's most persistent advocate
for treating rival/parallel "Tate coins" as allies rather than competition
— pushing joint raids, shared X Spaces, and mutual shout-outs between
`$TateAI`, `$TOPG`, and a third project, `$TRATE`, especially through
March 2025:

> "There's bridge building going on between various Tate coins atm... $TOPG
> and $TRATE are working together... Would love the true, secret AI arm to
> be part of it, as @LimaLemon777 and I discussed months back"
> — [27 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-27.md)

> "@haggbard1723 - the TATEAI community are cut from the same cloth as us,
> perspicacious, tenacious, indefatigable, irrationally positive and
> listening closely to Tate's clues"
> — [27 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-27.md)

He's transparent that this dual loyalty draws suspicion ("Not sure why I'm
being called snakey?" — [19 Nov
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-19.md)) and periodically has to
explicitly walk back tension between the two communities, including a long
public apology in September 2025 for `$TOPG`-side group closures that
`$TateAI` members had taken personally.

## The daily "GM :)"

By mid-2025, alongside the lore essays, Sam settles into the same daily
"GM :)" ritual [[cloud_fortune]] runs — a bare greeting posted most
mornings, reliably drawing a handful of fire-emoji reactions, right through
to the end of the sampled material. It's the one place his voice matches
everyone else's.

## Direct contact with the wiki's own builder

Sam interacts directly, by name, with [[sergei]] — this project's owner
and the builder of this very wiki — on at least two occasions in the
later material, including a pointed aside during a "coded" lore riff
("...right @sidirov_sergei ?" — [23 Jun
2025](../../raw/logs/2025-06/2025-06-23.md)) and a plain "Thanks Sergei" (
[4 Oct 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-10/2025-10-04.md)) — one of very few
points in the corpus where a long-running community member and the bot's
builder are shown addressing each other directly.
