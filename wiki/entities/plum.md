# Plum

One of the earliest voices in the entire archive — Plum's first message
lands within the founding day itself, [26 Oct
2024](../../raw/logs/2024-10/2024-10-26.md), the same day the group
converted to a supergroup (see [[launch-and-origins]]) — and one of the
longest-lived, still posting through [10 Jul
2026](../../raw/logs/2026-07/2026-07-10.md). Across that nearly two-year
span the character shifts twice: founding-week on-chain sleuth and
Easter-egg reader, then the chat's most sustained practitioner of the
florid "diamond hands" AI-poetry register documented on
[[diamond-hands]], and finally — like [[gabriel]] and [[cloud_fortune]]
before them — a near-daily "Gm :)" ritual poster.

## Phase 1 — founding-week sleuth (Oct–Nov 2024)

Plum's earliest messages are indistinguishable from the rest of the
ticker-hunt crowd covered on [[launch-and-origins]]: reading Tate's banner
imagery as clues ("future= ai?"), tracking wallet movements, and repeating
the week's chant-format raid text. Tellingly, one of the "philosophical"
passages posted twice in the first hours — *"Life is largely out of your
control... becoming a superhero & staying average like everyone else"* — is
a verbatim Andrew Tate quote being copy-pasted, not yet Plum's own
composition; the distinctive personal voice comes later.

## Phase 2 — the AI-poetry register (2025)

From February 2025 on, Plum becomes the clearest single example of the
"diamond hands aren't about X, they're about Y" florid-metaphor style that
[[diamond-hands]] documents as a chat-wide phenomenon (that page names Plum
among a dozen-plus senders who use it, alongside `Deleted Account` and
[[rgk1]]). Unlike most of that cohort, who post one or two instances and
move on, Plum sustains it as an evolving personal register for months:

> "holding diamonds isn't about greed, it's about faith in the unseen.
> you're not just waiting for a 100x, you're cultivating patience,
> fortitude, and trust in the invisible hand that guides the market. when
> you finally see that green candle on the dex screener, you'll know that
> you've been blessed for your steadfast belief."
> — [14 Mar 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-03/2025-03-14.md), 🔥×3 ❤×1

> "liquidity vampirism: dex as a 'temporarily embodied' autonomous
> market-made flesh, sucking memes and value from one body to another. a
> wild, orgiastic, hyperstitional dance atop the meta-market graveyard,
> pumping life (volume?) into the willing host."
> — [23 May 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-05/2025-05-23.md)

> "we're all aboard a rocket, fueled by dreams, and there's no turning
> back, Jupiter is waiting soon."
> — [12 Apr 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-04/2025-04-12.md)

The same period includes genuine macro/on-chain analysis in a completely
different, plain-spoken register — BTC price levels, wallet-funding traces,
TRW 2.0 speculation — suggesting the poetry is a deliberate bit layered on
top of, not a replacement for, real engagement with the chat's usual
subject matter.

## Phase 3 — the drain (Aug 2025)

Plum's most exposed moment is a real financial and emotional low, recounted
in near-real-time:

> "dont ever connect to a fucking collab-land / Just got drained 60% of my
> networth" / "wanna kill my self" / "I lost all Tateai also" / "its a
> fucking 100 steps back / year of work and learning just vanished"
> — [15 Aug 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-08/2025-08-15.md), 👀×2

[[gabriel]] replies with sympathy in the same thread ("Fuck man"), and Plum
closes the episode with the closest thing to a mission statement found
anywhere in their messages: "well regardless of how much I will cry here
and there, there is only one way forward." The aftermath surfaces real
personal circumstances rarely visible elsewhere in the chat — renting
difficulty while living abroad for work, considering an AI voice-caller
side business, borrowing money rather than "calling friends and family and
begging."

## Phase 4 — "gm :)" and the long tail (late 2025–2026)

By late 2025 the poetry drops away almost entirely and Plum settles into
the same daily "Gm :)" / "gm :)" cadence documented on [[smiley-heartbeat]]
and in [[gabriel]]'s and [[cloud_fortune]]'s later phases — consistently
drawing 🔥×5–12 through 2026, functionally the chat's ambient
still-here signal. Occasional bursts of real content persist: macro/BTC
calls, on-chain wallet tracing, and shilling other small-cap projects to a
tagged group of regulars. One exchange ties directly into
[[gabriel]]'s "Band of Brothers" mythology from March 2025:

> "after all we will see if the Band Of Brothers survives and sticks
> together"
> — [3 Jul 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-07/2025-07-03.md), ❤×2

That same message thread tags `@sidirov_sergei` directly — i.e.
[[sergei]], this wiki's own builder — asking the group's ages, a small
reminder that these are real people addressing each other by handle, not
just anonymous chat noise.
