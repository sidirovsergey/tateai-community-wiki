# Nikola

A launch-window raid coordinator and on-chain sleuth, active from
[3 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-03.md) through
[23 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-23.md) — a run of about eleven
weeks, entirely inside the chat's founding "which ticker is real" phase and
its immediate aftermath (see [[launch-and-origins]]), then nothing further
in the sampled material. Nikola posted his own X/Twitter link into the chat
more than once — `x.com/snajdarnikola` /
`x.com/SnajdarNikola` — which is where the alias above comes from; other
members separately refer to him in-chat as `@nikolaisnajdar`. He also
mentions being a first-year college student during this period
([4 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-04.md): "Im first year," after
joking about quitting college once the coin "blows up").

## Voice: raid captain and true believer

Nikola's default address is "Gs" (plural "guys"), and his core recurring
argument is a specific piece of lore-building: that the whole chat's
scavenger-hunt premise was designed on purpose and that `$TateAI`, not
`$TATE`, was always the intended answer:

> "its not tate, it was always tateai, from the day one. The whole point of
> this is to not trust ai's sayings rather think on your own. This whole
> group and community is assembled because of all those clues and sign
> which were given"
> — [6 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-06.md) (👍×5)

> "Tate will probably be pressured to do an EM abou the twhole tate
> terminal thing and be angry about people who aped in $TATE without
> looking at clear signs, riddles and hints which were give by a terminal
> itself. Followed by a reveal of a true ticker $Tateai which will send us
> much higher. Thats how i see this going further"
> — [8 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-08.md) (❤×10)

He's also one of the chat's most explicit voices framing the community
itself as the holding mechanism, reaching for the GameStop comparison
directly:

> "Gs, remember!!! Wen need to be like a GME investment cult. No one sold
> and gamestop stock skyrocketed"
> — [9 Nov 2024](../../raw/logs/2024-11/2024-11-09.md) (❤×2)

Like several other launch-era figures on this wiki, Nikola's casual
address routinely includes slurs alongside "Gs" and "bro" — worth stating
plainly rather than smoothing over, consistent with how this wiki treats
the same pattern on [[igelo]] and [[widelton]].

## Raid slogans and admin requests

A large share of Nikola's volume is the same slash-tag raid format used
chat-wide — stacked five-to-ten times in a row (`/The_ones_who_dont_raid_
are_GAY`, `/Faggots_sell_under_100M`, `/REAL_NIGGAS_RAID_HARD`). He also
repeatedly pushes for admin status for himself and other active members —
on [2 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-02.md): "I need an admin
man" — echoed the same day by a `Deleted Account` message naming Nikola
(by his `@nikolaisnajdar` handle) alongside [[rgk1]] and Ben as candidates.
It's never granted in the sampled material.

## Weighing in on FluxCapacitor

Nikola was active in the chat on the same day [[fluxcapacitor]] made one of
its rare appearances,
[2 Jan 2025](../../raw/logs/2025-01/2025-01-02.md), and joins the
speculation about who's behind the account — landing on a guess rather
than a confirmed answer:

> "FluxCapacitor is tate i think"
> — 2 Jan 2025

> "Gs, whats goin on, flux came in here and buying starts 😂. This is
> Andrew haahahah"
> — 2 Jan 2025 (❤×1)

Nothing in the raw material confirms this theory one way or the other — see
[[fluxcapacitor]]'s own page, which states plainly that the account's real
identity isn't determinable from chat content.
